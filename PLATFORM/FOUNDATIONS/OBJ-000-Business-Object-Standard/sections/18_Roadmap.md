# 18 — Roadmap Standard

---

# Purpose

The Roadmap Standard defines how the Business Object Standard will evolve over time.

Its purpose is to provide a structured vision for the continuous improvement of Business Objects, ensuring that Swissbay Nexus remains adaptable, scalable and aligned with future business and technology requirements.

The roadmap provides direction rather than implementation.

It communicates where the platform is heading.

---

# Objective

The objectives of this roadmap are to:

- guide future development
- maintain architectural consistency
- support controlled innovation
- prepare for emerging technologies
- improve governance maturity
- strengthen AI capabilities
- enable enterprise scalability

The roadmap provides long-term strategic direction for the Business Object Standard.

---

# Philosophy

Swissbay Nexus is designed to evolve continuously.

The Business Object Standard should remain stable while gradually expanding its capabilities.

Evolution should be:

- governed
- incremental
- backwards compatible where practical
- business driven
- technology independent

The framework should improve without disrupting existing Business Objects.

---

# Roadmap Principles

## Principle 1 — Business Driven

Future enhancements should solve genuine business problems.

Technology should support business strategy.

---

## Principle 2 — Modular Evolution

New capabilities should extend the framework rather than replace it.

Modules should remain independent wherever possible.

---

## Principle 3 — AI Native

Future enhancements should consider AI from the outset.

Every new capability should be explainable, governed and AI consumable.

---

## Principle 4 — Enterprise Scale

The framework should support:

- multiple companies
- multiple industries
- multiple countries
- multiple business units

without changing the core architecture.

---

## Principle 5 — Long-Term Stability

Core standards should remain stable while optional capabilities continue to evolve.

# Success Criteria

The roadmap will be considered successful when:

- every Business Object follows the standard
- AI understands every Business Object
- governance becomes largely automated
- documentation is generated automatically
- Business Objects become reusable across industries
- implementation effort is significantly reduced
- the platform scales across multiple organisations

---

# Governance

Roadmap changes require:

- Business Review
- Architecture Review
- Governance Approval
- Version Update

The roadmap should be reviewed annually or after major platform releases.

---

# Review Questions

Governance reviewers should ask:

- Does the roadmap align with the Swissbay Constitution?
- Does it support enterprise scalability?
- Does it preserve backwards compatibility?
- Are future capabilities governed?
- Does it strengthen the platform?

---

# Deliverable

The Business Object Standard shall maintain a living roadmap that guides the long-term evolution of Swissbay Nexus.

The roadmap should inspire innovation while ensuring architectural stability and governance.

---

# Standard Summary

The Roadmap Standard provides the strategic direction for the future evolution of the Business Object Standard and the wider Swissbay Nexus platform.

By defining phased growth, governance principles and long-term objectives, Swissbay ensures that the framework continues to mature without compromising consistency or business value.

The roadmap transforms the Business Object Standard from a static document into a continuously evolving architectural foundation capable of supporting the next generation of intelligent business operating systems.
