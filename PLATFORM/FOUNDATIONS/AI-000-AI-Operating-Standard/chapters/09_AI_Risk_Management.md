# 09 — AI Risk Management

## Purpose

AI risk must be identified and managed before automation becomes business-critical.

## Risk Types

- incorrect recommendation
- outdated context
- missing data
- biased reasoning
- over-automation
- customer communication risk
- financial exposure
- legal exposure
- security exposure

## Risk Controls

- human approval
- validation checks
- version control
- restricted tools
- logging
- role-based access
- review cycles
- escalation rules

## Rule

The higher the business impact, the stronger the human review requirement.
