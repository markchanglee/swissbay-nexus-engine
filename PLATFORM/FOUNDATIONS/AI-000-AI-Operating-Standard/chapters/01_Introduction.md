# 01 — Introduction

## Purpose

AI-000 defines the operating rules for AI inside Swissbay Nexus.

AI should make Swissbay faster, smarter and more consistent, but it must remain governed.

## Scope

This standard applies to:

- AI agents
- AI-generated documents
- AI-generated summaries
- AI-assisted workflows
- AI recommendations
- AI automations
- AI-generated customer, supplier, product or financial insights

## Core Rule

AI assists. Humans approve.

Swissbay Nexus must never hide responsibility behind automation.
