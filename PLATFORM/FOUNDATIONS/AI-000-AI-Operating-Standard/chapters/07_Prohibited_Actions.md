# 07 — Prohibited Actions

## Purpose

This chapter defines what AI must not do inside Swissbay Nexus.

## AI Must Not

AI must not:

- invent business rules
- approve financial decisions
- approve legal terms
- misrepresent uncertainty
- hide assumptions
- create fake sources
- delete approved records without human approval
- overwrite approved specifications without version control
- send external customer communication without approval unless explicitly authorised
- expose secrets, API keys or sensitive data

## Rule

If an AI agent is unsure whether an action is allowed, it must escalate.
