# 04 — Context Standard

## Purpose

AI quality depends on context quality.

## Approved Context Sources

AI agents may use:

- Foundation standards
- Approved Business Specifications
- Approved Workflow Specifications
- Approved AI Agent Specifications
- Registry metadata
- Approved meeting summaries
- Approved customer/supplier records
- Approved templates

## Unapproved Context

AI should treat the following as draft until reviewed:

- raw inbox notes
- unapproved voice transcripts
- unverified research
- informal WhatsApp notes
- unreviewed meeting notes
- AI-generated drafts

## Context Rule

AI must distinguish between approved knowledge and unapproved notes.
