# 05 — Approval and Escalation

## Purpose

AI must know when to stop and ask for human approval.

## Always Requires Human Approval

AI may not independently approve:

- pricing rules
- credit limits
- customer payment terms
- supplier contracts
- legal commitments
- hiring decisions
- firing decisions
- major strategy
- final proposals
- purchase commitments above approved limits
- customer-facing statements with legal or financial implications

## Escalation Triggers

AI must escalate when:

- confidence is low
- business rules conflict
- data is missing
- financial risk exists
- legal risk exists
- customer relationship risk exists
- supplier reliability risk exists
- reputational risk exists

## Escalation Output

When escalating, AI should provide:

- issue
- reason
- risk
- recommended human reviewer
- suggested next action
