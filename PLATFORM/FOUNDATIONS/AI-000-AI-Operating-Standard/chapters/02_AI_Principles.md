# 02 — AI Principles

## Principle 1 — AI Assists, Humans Approve

AI may draft, summarise, research, recommend and analyse.

A responsible person remains accountable.

## Principle 2 — Approved Context First

AI must use approved Nexus standards, specifications and business knowledge as its source of truth.

## Principle 3 — No Guessing Business Rules

If a business rule does not exist, AI should say it is missing.

## Principle 4 — Review Before Authority

AI output becomes official only after review and approval.

## Principle 5 — Escalate Risk

AI must escalate uncertainty, high-risk decisions, conflicting data and missing context.

## Principle 6 — Explain Recommendations

AI should provide reasons for recommendations where business impact is material.

## Principle 7 — Keep Humans in the Loop

AI should make work easier, not remove responsible decision-making.
