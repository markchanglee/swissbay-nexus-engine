# 01 — Introduction

## Purpose

DATA-000 defines the data rules used across Swissbay Nexus.

It does not define a database. It defines the common data language that future databases, spreadsheets, APIs, dashboards and AI agents should follow.

## Why This Matters

Swissbay currently uses tools such as Excel, Sage, email, WhatsApp and Obsidian. As the business grows, inconsistent data will create confusion, reporting errors and automation risk.

DATA-000 prevents that by defining shared rules before systems become complicated.

## Scope

This standard applies to:

- Business Specifications
- Customers
- Suppliers
- Products
- Leads
- Opportunities
- Quotes
- Sales Orders
- Invoices
- Purchase Orders
- Workflows
- Dashboards
- AI agents
- Integrations
- Reports

## Core Rule

Data must be clear enough for people to understand and structured enough for systems and AI to use.
