# OBJ-009 — Opportunity

## Overview

The Opportunity Business Object defines the governed representation of every potential revenue opportunity managed within Swissbay Nexus.

An Opportunity represents a qualified commercial engagement with a prospective or existing customer that has the potential to result in a quotation, contract, project, subscription or sales order.

The Opportunity Business Object provides the single source of truth for opportunity identity, commercial value, sales stage, probability, stakeholders, expected close date and pipeline governance.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Opportunity Business Object is to establish one canonical representation of every sales opportunity across Swissbay Nexus.

Opportunities may include:

- Product Sales
- Service Engagements
- Consulting Projects
- SaaS Opportunities
- Subscription Renewals
- Cross-Sell Opportunities
- Up-Sell Opportunities
- Government Tenders
- Mining Contracts
- Strategic Partnerships
- Framework Agreements
- Enterprise Programmes

Every Opportunity follows the same governed commercial lifecycle regardless of industry or sales methodology.
