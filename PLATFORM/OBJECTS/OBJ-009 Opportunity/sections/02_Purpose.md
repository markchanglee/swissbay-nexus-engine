# 02 — Purpose

---

# Overview

The Opportunity Business Object exists to provide a single, governed representation of every commercial opportunity throughout its sales lifecycle.

It enables organisations to manage potential revenue consistently while improving forecasting, collaboration, governance and commercial execution.

The Opportunity Business Object standardises opportunity management across departments, business units and technology platforms.

---

# Why the Opportunity Business Object Exists

Organisations frequently manage opportunities using disconnected spreadsheets, emails or isolated CRM systems.

Without a governed Opportunity Business Object they commonly experience:

- fragmented sales pipelines
- duplicate opportunities
- inconsistent qualification
- unreliable forecasts
- poor pipeline visibility
- weak governance
- missed follow-up activities
- inconsistent executive reporting
- inaccurate revenue projections

The Opportunity Business Object addresses these challenges by providing one canonical enterprise model for opportunity governance.

# Scope of Responsibility

The Opportunity Business Object governs:

- opportunity identity
- sales stage
- commercial value
- probability
- ownership
- expected close date
- commercial activities
- opportunity history
- forecasting information

Contracts, Projects and Sales Orders remain governed by their respective Business Objects.
