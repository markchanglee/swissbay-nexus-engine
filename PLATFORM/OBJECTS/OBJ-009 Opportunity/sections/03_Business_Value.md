# 03 — Business Value

---

# Overview

The Opportunity Business Object delivers measurable business value by providing a governed framework for managing commercial opportunities throughout their lifecycle.

Every Opportunity represents potential future revenue. By governing how opportunities are qualified, managed, forecasted and converted, Swissbay Nexus enables organisations to improve sales performance, increase forecast accuracy and strengthen commercial governance.

The Opportunity Business Object transforms sales pipelines into trusted enterprise assets.

---

# Purpose

The Business Value Model explains how governed opportunity management improves organisational performance.

It demonstrates how commercial opportunities contribute measurable financial, operational and strategic value.

Business value should always be measurable, sustainable and aligned with organisational objectives.
