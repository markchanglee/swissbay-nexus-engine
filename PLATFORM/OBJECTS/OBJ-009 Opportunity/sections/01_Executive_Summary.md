# 01 — Executive Summary

---

# Overview

The Opportunity Business Object defines the governed representation of every potential commercial engagement managed within Swissbay Nexus.

An Opportunity represents a qualified business prospect with the potential to generate revenue through products, services, subscriptions, projects or strategic partnerships.

The Opportunity Business Object serves as the authoritative source of truth for opportunity identity, commercial value, sales stage, probability, ownership, expected close date and pipeline governance.

Unlike the Customer Business Object, which represents an organisation or individual, the Opportunity Business Object represents a specific revenue-generating engagement associated with that customer.

---

# Business Purpose

The Opportunity Business Object establishes one governed representation of every commercial opportunity across the enterprise.

It standardises:

- opportunity identity
- opportunity ownership
- sales stages
- commercial value
- revenue probability
- expected close dates
- pipeline reporting
- opportunity governance

Every Opportunity follows one governed commercial lifecycle regardless of industry or sales methodology.
