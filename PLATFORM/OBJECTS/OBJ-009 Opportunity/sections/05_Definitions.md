# 05 — Definitions

---

# Overview

This document defines the official business terminology used throughout the Opportunity Business Object.

These definitions establish a common enterprise vocabulary ensuring that Sales, Business Development, Marketing, Finance, Legal, Executive Management, Artificial Intelligence and enterprise systems interpret opportunity information consistently.

These definitions are the authoritative terminology for opportunity governance within Swissbay Nexus.

---

# Core Definitions

## Opportunity

A governed commercial engagement with a prospective or existing customer that has the potential to generate future revenue.

Every Opportunity is uniquely represented by one Opportunity Business Object.

---

## Opportunity Identifier

The permanent globally unique identifier assigned to every Opportunity.

Example

```
OPP-000458
```

Opportunity Identifiers:

- never change
- are never reused
- uniquely identify an Opportunity

---

## Opportunity Name

The approved business name describing the commercial engagement.

Example

```
Anglo Platinum Asset Management Programme
```

---

## Opportunity Type

The commercial classification describing the nature of the opportunity.

Examples include:

- Product Sale
- Consulting Engagement
- Managed Service
- Subscription
- Renewal
- Cross-Sell
- Up-Sell
- Government Tender
- Mining Contract
- Strategic Partnership

---

## Opportunity Owner

The employee accountable for managing the opportunity throughout its lifecycle.

The Opportunity Owner is responsible for commercial execution and customer engagement.

---

## Sales Stage

The governed commercial stage representing the current progress of an Opportunity.

Examples include:

- Qualification
- Discovery
- Proposal
- Negotiation
- Verbal Agreement
- Contract Review
- Closed Won
- Closed Lost

---

## Pipeline

The governed collection of active Opportunities progressing through the sales lifecycle.

Pipeline value supports revenue forecasting and executive planning.

---

## Estimated Value

The projected commercial value of the Opportunity before contractual commitment.

Estimated Value supports forecasting but does not represent recognised revenue.

---

## Win Probability

The governed percentage representing the likelihood of successfully closing an Opportunity.

Probability is used for weighted revenue forecasting.

---

## Expected Close Date

The projected date on which the Opportunity is expected to reach a final commercial outcome.

---

## Forecast Category

The commercial confidence classification used in executive forecasting.

Examples include:

- Pipeline
- Best Case
- Commit
- Closed

---

## Next Best Action

The recommended commercial activity most likely to improve the probability of success.

Recommendations may be generated by users or Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Competitor

A competing organisation actively pursuing the same commercial opportunity.

Competitor information supports commercial strategy and win/loss analysis.

---

## Win/Loss Reason

The documented business reason explaining why an Opportunity was successfully won or lost.

This information contributes to organisational learning.

---

## Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence refers to governed capabilities that analyse opportunity information to generate predictions, recommendations and commercial insights.

AI supports sales teams but never replaces commercial accountability.

# Terminology Rules

Throughout the Opportunity Business Object:

- Use **Opportunity Identifier** rather than Opportunity ID.
- Use **Opportunity Owner** for commercial accountability.
- Use **Sales Stage** for lifecycle progression.
- Use **Estimated Value** rather than Expected Revenue.
- Use **Win Probability** for forecasting.

---

# AI Interpretation

Artificial Intelligence must interpret Opportunity terminology according to this document.

Where ambiguity exists, these definitions take precedence over external terminology.

---

# Definition Governance

Changes to opportunity terminology require:

- Sales Review
- Enterprise Architecture Review
- Governance Approval
- Version Update

# Definitions Summary

This document establishes the official enterprise vocabulary for the Opportunity Business Object.

By defining consistent terminology for commercial governance, forecasting, customer engagement and revenue management, Swissbay Nexus ensures that people, systems and Artificial Intelligence operate from a common understanding.
