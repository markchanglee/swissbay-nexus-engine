# 19 — Version History

---

# Overview

The Version History records the controlled evolution of the Contract Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

It provides a permanent governance record of structural, semantic, legal and architectural changes made throughout the lifecycle of the Contract Business Object.

Every published version represents an approved enterprise standard.

---

# Purpose

The Version History exists to:

- document Business Object evolution
- preserve governance decisions
- support legal audit requirements
- communicate platform improvements
- maintain backwards compatibility
- improve architectural transparency

Version History forms part of the permanent governance record.

---

# Versioning Standard

The Contract Business Object follows Semantic Versioning.

```text
MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH
```

Example

```text
2.1.3
```

| Version Element | Meaning |
|-----------------|---------|
| Major | Breaking business or structural changes |
| Minor | New capabilities with backwards compatibility |
| Patch | Corrections, documentation and governance updates |

# Breaking Change Policy

Breaking changes require:

- Business Impact Assessment
- Legal Review
- Architecture Review
- Integration Assessment
- Governance Approval
- Migration Strategy
- Communication Plan

Breaking changes should be minimised wherever possible.

---

# Backwards Compatibility

Minor and patch releases should remain backwards compatible.

Compatibility applies to:

- APIs
- workflows
- Business Rules
- dashboards
- KPIs
- automation
- integrations
- AI models

Migration guidance must accompany breaking changes.

# Retirement Policy

If the Contract Business Object is superseded:

- a successor Business Object must be identified
- migration guidance must be published
- historical Contracts remain accessible
- audit obligations continue
- archived versions remain available

Enterprise legal history must always be preserved.

The Version History provides the official governance record for the Contract Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

By documenting every approved enhancement, governance decision and architectural evolution, Swissbay ensures that the Contract Business Object remains transparent, auditable and maintainable throughout its lifecycle while supporting enterprise agreement management, intelligent automation and continuous organisational improvement.
