# 11 — Department Usage

---

# Overview

The Contract Business Object is an enterprise capability shared across multiple business functions.

Although Legal owns contractual governance, Contracts support commercial execution, procurement, finance, project delivery, compliance and executive oversight.

This document defines departmental responsibilities and governance boundaries throughout the Contract Lifecycle.

---

# Purpose

The Department Usage Model exists to:

- define departmental responsibilities
- strengthen governance
- improve collaboration
- eliminate ownership ambiguity
- improve accountability
- support Artificial Intelligence
- enable enterprise-wide agreement management

Contracts are shared enterprise assets governed through clearly defined responsibilities.

---

# Department Participation

Each department performs governed activities throughout the Contract Lifecycle.
