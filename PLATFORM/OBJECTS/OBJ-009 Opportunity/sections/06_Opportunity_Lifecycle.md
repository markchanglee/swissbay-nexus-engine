# 06 — Opportunity Lifecycle

---

# Overview

The Opportunity Lifecycle defines the governed progression of every Opportunity from initial identification through commercial closure.

Every Opportunity follows a standard lifecycle that ensures consistent governance, forecasting accuracy, customer engagement and executive visibility.

An Opportunity exists in one—and only one—lifecycle stage at any given time.

---

# Purpose

The Opportunity Lifecycle exists to:

- standardise commercial governance
- improve forecasting accuracy
- strengthen sales execution
- support Artificial Intelligence
- improve customer engagement
- maximise commercial success

The lifecycle provides one operating model for enterprise opportunity management.

---

# Lifecycle Overview

Every Opportunity progresses through the following stages.

```text
Identified

↓

Qualified

↓

Discovery

↓

Solution Development

↓

Proposal Submitted

↓

Negotiation

↓

Commercial Review

↓

Closed Won / Closed Lost

↓

Archived
```

Opportunities normally spend the greatest amount of time between **Discovery** and **Negotiation**.

---

# Stage 1 — Identified

## Purpose

Capture a potential commercial opportunity.

### Activities

- record opportunity
- identify customer
- assign owner
- perform initial assessment

### Exit Criteria

Opportunity registered.

---

# Stage 2 — Qualified

## Purpose

Determine whether the opportunity should enter the active pipeline.

### Activities

- customer qualification
- budget assessment
- authority validation
- need analysis
- timing assessment

### Outputs

Qualified Opportunity.

---

# Stage 3 — Discovery

## Purpose

Understand customer requirements.

### Activities

- workshops
- stakeholder interviews
- requirements gathering
- competitor assessment
- opportunity sizing

### Outputs

Documented customer requirements.

---

# Stage 4 — Solution Development

## Purpose

Develop the proposed commercial solution.

### Activities

- solution design
- pricing
- commercial modelling
- internal approvals

### Outputs

Approved commercial solution.

---

# Stage 5 — Proposal Submitted

## Purpose

Present the commercial proposal.

### Activities

- submit proposal
- customer review
- clarify questions
- update forecast

### Outputs

Customer evaluation underway.

---

# Stage 6 — Negotiation

## Purpose

Reach commercial agreement.

### Activities

- commercial negotiations
- pricing discussions
- contract review
- legal engagement
- executive approvals

### Outputs

Commercial agreement pending.

---

# Stage 7 — Commercial Review

## Purpose

Prepare for final commercial outcome.

### Activities

- confirm approvals
- final pricing
- legal validation
- delivery readiness

### Outputs

Opportunity ready for closure.

---

# Stage 8 — Closed Won / Closed Lost

## Closed Won

Activities include:

- convert to Contract
- generate Sales Order
- initiate Project (where applicable)
- update revenue forecast

## Closed Lost

Activities include:

- record loss reason
- identify competitor
- capture lessons learned
- notify stakeholders

Opportunity history remains permanently governed.

---

# Stage 9 — Archived

## Purpose

Retain historical commercial information.

Archived Opportunities:

- remain searchable
- support reporting
- support AI learning
- support audit requirements
- cannot be modified without governance approval

---

# Lifecycle Events

Major lifecycle events include:

- Opportunity Created
- Opportunity Qualified
- Proposal Submitted
- Negotiation Started
- Commercial Review Approved
- Opportunity Won
- Opportunity Lost
- Opportunity Archived

These events trigger workflows, automation and dashboards.

---

# AI Throughout the Lifecycle

Artificial Intelligence may:

- predict win probability
- recommend next best actions
- identify stalled opportunities
- forecast revenue
- analyse customer sentiment
- recommend executive intervention

AI remains advisory throughout the lifecycle.

---

# Governance

Every lifecycle transition requires:

- validation
- approval where applicable
- audit logging
- workflow execution
- Business Rule compliance

The Swissbay Governance Engine enforces lifecycle integrity.

# Lifecycle Summary

The Opportunity Lifecycle establishes the governed progression of every commercial engagement from identification through archival.

By defining controlled lifecycle stages, governance checkpoints, commercial transitions and AI-assisted forecasting, Swissbay Nexus ensures consistent opportunity management, stronger forecasting accuracy and complete commercial traceability.
