# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Contract Roadmap defines the long-term strategic evolution of the Contract Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

It provides a structured vision for advancing enterprise agreement management through intelligent automation, Artificial Intelligence, knowledge graph integration, digital contracts and predictive governance while maintaining legal integrity, interoperability and backwards compatibility.

The roadmap ensures Contracts continue to evolve alongside enterprise capabilities without compromising governance.

---

# Purpose

The Contract Roadmap exists to:

- provide strategic direction
- improve enterprise contract management
- strengthen legal governance
- support Artificial Intelligence
- improve automation
- enable enterprise intelligence
- support organisational growth
- maximise contractual value

The roadmap guides controlled and measurable evolution.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Future enhancements should always be:

- business-driven
- legally governed
- reusable
- secure
- measurable
- AI-ready
- event-driven
- backwards compatible

Every enhancement should improve measurable business outcomes.
