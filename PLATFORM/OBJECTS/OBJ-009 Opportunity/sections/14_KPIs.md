# 14 — Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

---

# Overview

The Contract KPI Model defines the official performance measurements used to evaluate enterprise contract management within Swissbay Nexus.

KPIs provide consistent, governed metrics for measuring legal performance, commercial effectiveness, operational execution, compliance, renewals and enterprise risk.

Every KPI follows the Swissbay KPI Standard (KPI-000).

---

# Purpose

The Contract KPI Model exists to:

- measure contract performance
- strengthen governance
- improve compliance
- optimise renewals
- improve operational execution
- support Artificial Intelligence
- reduce enterprise risk
- maximise contractual value

KPIs measure business outcomes rather than operational activity.

# KPI-CON-001 — Active Contract Value

## Formula

```text
Sum of Contract Values

for all Active Contracts
```

Measures the total commercial value governed by active Contracts.

---

# KPI-CON-002 — Contract Cycle Time

## Formula

```text
Contract Activation Date

−

Contract Creation Date
```

Measures the average duration required to move a Contract from creation to activation.

---

# KPI-CON-003 — Renewal Rate

## Formula

```text
Renewed Contracts

÷

Eligible Contracts

×

100
```

Measures renewal success.

---

# KPI-CON-004 — Obligation Compliance

## Formula

```text
Completed Obligations

÷

Total Obligations

×

100
```

Measures contractual fulfilment.

---

# KPI-CON-005 — SLA Compliance

## Formula

```text
SLA Targets Achieved

÷

Total SLA Targets

×

100
```

Measures contractual service performance.

---

# KPI-CON-006 — Contract Risk Score

Measures overall contractual exposure using governed risk factors including:

- legal complexity
- commercial exposure
- financial exposure
- supplier dependency
- customer dependency
- compliance status

Risk Scores are generated using approved governance rules.

---

# KPI-CON-007 — Amendment Frequency

## Formula

```text
Total Amendments

÷

Active Contracts
```

Measures contractual stability.

High amendment frequency may indicate poor initial contract quality or changing business requirements.

---

# KPI-CON-008 — Contract Approval Time

## Formula

```text
Approval Date

−

Submission Date
```

Measures approval efficiency.

---

# KPI-CON-009 — Compliance Rate

## Formula

```text
Compliant Contracts

÷

Total Active Contracts

×

100
```

Measures regulatory and policy compliance.

---

# KPI-CON-010 — AI Recommendation Accuracy

Measures the effectiveness of AI recommendations by comparing:

- predicted risks vs actual outcomes
- renewal predictions vs actual renewals
- clause recommendations vs accepted changes
- obligation predictions vs verified obligations

Supports continuous AI improvement.

Before approval verify:

- KPI purpose documented
- calculation defined
- owner assigned
- reporting frequency defined
- measurable business outcome demonstrated

# KPI Summary

The Contract KPI Portfolio provides the official measurements for evaluating enterprise agreement management within Swissbay Nexus.

By defining consistent, governed and measurable indicators, Swissbay enables organisations to improve legal governance, strengthen operational execution, optimise commercial performance and maximise the long-term value of enterprise agreements.
