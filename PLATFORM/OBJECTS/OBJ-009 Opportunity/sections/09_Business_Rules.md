# 09 — Business Rules

---

# Overview

The Business Rules define the governance policies controlling every Contract throughout its lifecycle.

These rules ensure Contracts are created, reviewed, negotiated, approved, executed, monitored and archived consistently while maintaining legal integrity, commercial governance and regulatory compliance.

Business Rules define what is permitted.

Validation confirms whether those rules have been satisfied.

---

# Purpose

The Contract Business Rules exist to:

- standardise agreement governance
- strengthen legal compliance
- improve commercial consistency
- reduce contractual risk
- improve operational accountability
- support Artificial Intelligence
- maintain auditability

Every Contract interaction must comply with these rules.

---

# Rule Categories

Contract Business Rules are organised into:

- Identity Rules
- Ownership Rules
- Legal Rules
- Commercial Rules
- Financial Rules
- Operational Rules
- Lifecycle Rules
- Security Rules
- AI Rules
- Audit Rules

---

# Identity Rules

## BR-CON-001

Every Contract must have one unique Contract Identifier.

---

## BR-CON-002

Contract Identifiers:

- cannot be modified
- cannot be reused
- remain permanent

---

## BR-CON-003

Every Contract must contain:

- Contract Identifier
- Contract Type
- Contract Owner
- Contract Parties
- Effective Date
- Contract Status

---

## BR-CON-004

Duplicate Contracts are prohibited.

Duplicate detection should consider:

- Contract Number
- Contract Parties
- Effective Date
- Contract Type

---

# Ownership Rules

## BR-CON-005

Every Contract must have one assigned Contract Owner.

---

## BR-CON-006

Ownership changes require:

- previous owner
- new owner
- business justification
- audit history

---

## BR-CON-007

Contract Owners remain accountable until ownership is formally transferred.

---

# Legal Rules

## BR-CON-008

Contracts may only become Active after legal approval.

---

## BR-CON-009

Signed Contracts are legally immutable.

Only governed Amendments may modify contractual commitments.

---

## BR-CON-010

Every Amendment becomes part of permanent Contract history.

---

# Commercial Rules

## BR-CON-011

Commercial terms require delegated approval before signature.

---

## BR-CON-012

Pricing schedules must remain consistent with approved commercial policies.

---

## BR-CON-013

Projects and Sales Orders must reference a governing Contract where applicable.

---

# Financial Rules

## BR-CON-014

Payment milestones must be explicitly defined before Contract activation.

---

## BR-CON-015

Contract Values must always include a governing currency.

---

# Operational Rules

## BR-CON-016

Every contractual obligation must have:

- owner
- due date
- completion status

---

## BR-CON-017

Milestones may trigger workflows, notifications and automation.

---

# Lifecycle Rules

## BR-CON-018

Contracts must follow the approved Contract Lifecycle.

---

## BR-CON-019

Lifecycle stages may only progress through approved transitions.

---

## BR-CON-020

Archived Contracts may not be modified without governance approval.

---

# Security Rules

## BR-CON-021

Contract access follows role-based security.

---

## BR-CON-022

Commercial values, pricing and legal clauses must be protected according to enterprise security policies.

---

# AI Rules

## BR-CON-023

Artificial Intelligence may:

- classify clauses
- compare contracts
- predict renewals
- monitor obligations
- recommend improvements

---

## BR-CON-024

Artificial Intelligence may not:

- approve contracts
- modify legal clauses
- sign agreements
- override governance

Human accountability remains mandatory.

---

# Audit Rules

## BR-CON-025

Every significant Contract change records:

- user
- timestamp
- previous value
- new value
- business reason

---

## BR-CON-026

Amendments, approvals, signatures and lifecycle transitions must remain permanently auditable.

---

# Rule Enforcement

Business Rules are enforced through:

- Validation Engine
- Workflow Engine
- Security Engine
- Automation Engine
- API Gateway
- AI Governance Engine

Rules remain technology independent.

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions require:

- documented justification
- executive approval
- legal approval
- governance review
- expiry date

Temporary exceptions must never become permanent operating behaviour.

---

# Business Rules Summary

The Contract Business Rules establish the governance framework for enterprise agreement management within Swissbay Nexus.

By defining consistent rules for legal governance, commercial execution, financial commitments, operational obligations, Artificial Intelligence and auditability, Swissbay ensures every Contract remains trusted, enforceable and fully governed throughout its lifecycle.
