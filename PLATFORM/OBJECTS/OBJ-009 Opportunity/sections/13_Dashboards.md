# 13 — Dashboards

---

# Overview

The Contract Dashboard Portfolio provides operational, tactical and executive visibility into enterprise agreements managed within Swissbay Nexus.

Dashboards transform governed Contract information into actionable intelligence supporting Legal, Sales, Procurement, Finance, Operations, Compliance, Executive Management and Artificial Intelligence.

Dashboards consume Contract information.

They never own or modify Contract data.

---

# Purpose

Contract dashboards exist to:

- monitor contract portfolios
- improve compliance
- reduce contractual risk
- strengthen governance
- improve executive visibility
- optimise renewals
- support Artificial Intelligence

Dashboards answer business questions rather than simply displaying information.

# AI Integration

Artificial Intelligence enhances dashboards by:

- identifying risk
- forecasting renewals
- prioritising obligations
- detecting anomalies
- generating executive summaries

---

# Dashboard Governance

Every dashboard should:

- consume governed Contract information
- display governed KPIs
- respect role-based security
- maintain auditability
- preserve consistent business definitions

Dashboards never redefine business calculations.

---

# Dashboard Summary

The Contract Dashboard Portfolio transforms governed Contract information into enterprise intelligence.

By combining portfolio visibility, compliance monitoring, renewal forecasting, operational obligations and AI-powered recommendations, Swissbay Nexus enables organisations to maximise the value of enterprise agreements while reducing legal, commercial and operational risk.
