# 09 — Business Rules

---

# Overview

The Business Rules define the governance policies controlling every Asset throughout its lifecycle.

These rules ensure that Assets are acquired, commissioned, operated, maintained, transferred, retired and disposed of consistently while maintaining financial accountability, operational reliability and regulatory compliance.

Business Rules define what is permitted.

Validation confirms whether those rules have been satisfied.

---

# Purpose

The Asset Business Rules exist to:

- standardise asset governance
- strengthen accountability
- improve lifecycle management
- reduce operational risk
- support financial accuracy
- enable Artificial Intelligence
- maintain complete auditability

Every Asset interaction must comply with these rules.

---

# Rule Categories

Asset Business Rules are organised into:

- Identity Rules
- Ownership Rules
- Lifecycle Rules
- Financial Rules
- Maintenance Rules
- Operational Rules
- Security Rules
- AI Rules
- Audit Rules

---

# Identity Rules

## BR-ASSET-001

Every Asset must have one unique Asset Identifier.

---

## BR-ASSET-002

Asset Identifiers:

- cannot be modified
- cannot be reused
- remain permanent

---

## BR-ASSET-003

Every Asset must contain:

- Asset Identifier
- Asset Name
- Asset Category
- Asset Status
- Business Owner
- Current Location

---

## BR-ASSET-004

Duplicate Assets are prohibited.

Duplicate detection should consider:

- Serial Number
- Manufacturer
- Model
- Asset Name
- Purchase Order

---

# Ownership Rules

## BR-ASSET-005

Every Asset must have one Business Owner.

---

## BR-ASSET-006

Operational Custodians may change throughout the Asset Lifecycle, but Business Ownership must always be maintained.

---

# Lifecycle Rules

## BR-ASSET-007

Assets must progress through the approved Asset Lifecycle.

---

## BR-ASSET-008

Retired Assets may not return to Active Operation without formal governance approval.

---

## BR-ASSET-009

Disposed Assets become permanently read-only.

---

# Financial Rules

## BR-ASSET-010

Capital Assets must record:

- Acquisition Cost
- Acquisition Date
- Depreciation Method

---

## BR-ASSET-011

Asset valuations may only be modified by authorised Finance personnel.

---

## BR-ASSET-012

Asset disposal requires financial reconciliation before completion.

---

# Maintenance Rules

## BR-ASSET-013

Maintenance history must remain permanently linked to the Asset.

---

## BR-ASSET-014

Critical Assets must maintain approved preventive maintenance schedules.

---

## BR-ASSET-015

Safety inspections must be completed before returning an Asset to Active Operation where regulatory requirements apply.

---

# Operational Rules

## BR-ASSET-016

Every Asset location change must be recorded.

---

## BR-ASSET-017

Assets assigned to Projects must maintain references to the governing Project.

---

## BR-ASSET-018

Assets under maintenance may not be allocated to operational activities.

---

# Security Rules

## BR-ASSET-019

Asset access follows role-based security.

---

## BR-ASSET-020

Financial information is restricted to authorised users.

---

# AI Rules

## BR-ASSET-021

Artificial Intelligence may:

- forecast failures
- predict Remaining Useful Life
- optimise maintenance schedules
- analyse utilisation
- recommend replacement

---

## BR-ASSET-022

Artificial Intelligence may not:

- approve purchases
- approve disposal
- modify financial values
- override governance
- bypass Business Rules

Human accountability remains mandatory.

---

# Audit Rules

## BR-ASSET-023

Every significant Asset change must record:

- user
- timestamp
- previous value
- new value
- business reason

---

## BR-ASSET-024

Ownership changes, valuation changes, lifecycle transitions and disposal events must always be auditable.

---

# Rule Priorities

| Priority | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Critical | Operation blocked |
| High | Immediate correction required |
| Medium | Warning issued |
| Low | Advisory only |

# Rule Enforcement

Business Rules are enforced through:

- Validation Engine
- Workflow Engine
- Security Engine
- Automation Engine
- API Gateway
- AI Governance Engine

Rules remain independent of implementation technology.

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions require:

- documented business justification
- executive approval
- governance review
- expiry date
- audit record

Temporary exceptions must never become permanent operating behaviour.

---

# Business Rules Summary

The Asset Business Rules establish the governance framework for enterprise asset management within Swissbay Nexus.

By defining consistent rules for identity, ownership, maintenance, financial control, lifecycle progression, Artificial Intelligence and auditability, Swissbay ensures that Assets remain trusted, compliant and operationally effective throughout their lifecycle.
