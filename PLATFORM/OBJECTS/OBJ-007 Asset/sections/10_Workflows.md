# 10 — Workflows

---

# Overview

The Workflow Model defines the governed business processes that manage Assets throughout their lifecycle.

Workflows coordinate acquisition, commissioning, maintenance, inspections, transfers, retirement and disposal while ensuring compliance with Business Rules, Security policies and governance standards.

Every workflow is event-driven and fully auditable.

---

# Purpose

The Asset Workflow Model exists to:

- standardise asset operations
- enforce governance
- automate approvals
- improve collaboration
- strengthen accountability
- improve reporting
- enable Artificial Intelligence

Workflows transform asset governance into repeatable business processes.

---

# Workflow Principles

Asset workflows follow these principles:

- business-first
- event-driven
- governed
- auditable
- secure
- reusable
- explainable

Workflows describe business behaviour rather than system implementation.

# WF-ASSET-001 — Asset Registration

## Trigger

Approved Asset Acquisition

## Workflow

1. Create Asset record
2. Assign Asset Identifier
3. Capture ownership
4. Assign classification
5. Record acquisition details
6. Create audit record

---

# WF-ASSET-002 — Asset Acquisition

## Trigger

Approved Capital Request

## Workflow

1. Validate business case
2. Verify budget
3. Select supplier
4. Execute procurement
5. Receive asset
6. Register Asset

4. Record commissioning results

# Workflow Events

Major workflow events include:

- Asset Registered
- Asset Assigned
- Maintenance Completed
- Inspection Failed
- Asset Transferred
- Asset Retired
- Asset Disposed

These events trigger automation, dashboards and notifications.

---

# AI Workflow Support

Artificial Intelligence may:

- recommend maintenance timing
- prioritise inspections
- identify failure trends
- recommend replacement
- forecast maintenance demand

AI recommendations support, but never replace, governed workflows.

---

# Workflow Governance

All Asset workflows require:

- validation
- Business Rule compliance
- role-based security
- audit logging
- lifecycle integrity

The Swissbay Workflow Engine enforces workflow execution.

# Workflow Summary

The Asset Workflow Model transforms enterprise asset management into a governed, repeatable and auditable operating model.

By coordinating acquisition, maintenance, inspections, transfers, retirement and disposal through structured workflows, Swissbay Nexus ensures that every Asset is managed consistently, securely and efficiently throughout its lifecycle.
