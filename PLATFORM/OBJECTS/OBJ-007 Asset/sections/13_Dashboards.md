# 13 — Dashboards

---

# Overview

The Asset Dashboard Portfolio provides operational, tactical and executive visibility into enterprise assets across Swissbay Nexus.

Dashboards transform governed Asset information into actionable intelligence that supports operations, maintenance, finance, executive governance and Artificial Intelligence.

Dashboards consume Asset information.

They never own or modify Asset data.

---

# Purpose

Asset dashboards exist to:

- monitor asset health
- improve operational visibility
- strengthen governance
- optimise maintenance
- improve investment planning
- support executive decision-making
- enable Artificial Intelligence

Dashboards answer business questions rather than simply displaying information.

# DASH-ASSET-001 — Asset Overview Dashboard

## Purpose

Provides operational visibility into individual assets.

### Business Questions

- What is the current Asset Status?
- Where is the Asset located?
- Who owns the Asset?
- What is the Asset Health Score?
- When is the next maintenance activity due?

### KPIs

- Asset Health Score
- Current Condition
- Remaining Useful Life
- Maintenance Compliance
- Utilisation Rate

# AI Integration

Artificial Intelligence enhances dashboards by:

- predicting failures
- identifying anomalies
- forecasting replacement
- recommending maintenance
- generating executive summaries

Every recommendation includes supporting evidence and confidence scores.

---

# Dashboard Governance

Every dashboard should:

- consume governed Asset information
- display governed KPIs
- respect role-based security
- maintain auditability
- preserve consistent business definitions

Dashboards never redefine business calculations.

---

# Dashboard Summary

The Asset Dashboard Portfolio transforms governed Asset information into enterprise intelligence.

By combining operational visibility, maintenance monitoring, financial oversight, utilisation analysis and AI-powered insights, Swissbay Nexus enables organisations to maximise asset value while maintaining governance, reliability and operational excellence.
