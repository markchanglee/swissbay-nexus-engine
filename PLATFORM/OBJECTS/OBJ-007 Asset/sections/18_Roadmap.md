# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Asset Roadmap defines the long-term strategic evolution of the Asset Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

It provides a structured vision for enhancing lifecycle management, predictive maintenance, digital twins, Artificial Intelligence and enterprise integrations while maintaining governance, interoperability and backwards compatibility.

---

# Purpose

The Asset Roadmap exists to:

- provide strategic direction
- strengthen enterprise asset management
- improve operational reliability
- enable Artificial Intelligence
- support enterprise growth
- improve sustainability
- maximise asset value

The roadmap guides controlled evolution.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Future enhancements should always be:

- business-driven
- reusable
- governed
- secure
- measurable
- AI-ready
- backwards compatible

Every enhancement should improve enterprise asset management.

# Roadmap Summary

The Asset Roadmap provides the long-term strategic vision for enterprise asset management within Swissbay Nexus.

By progressively introducing Digital Twins, IoT connectivity, predictive maintenance and intelligent lifecycle optimisation, Swissbay ensures that the Asset Business Object evolves while remaining governed, secure and strategically aligned.
