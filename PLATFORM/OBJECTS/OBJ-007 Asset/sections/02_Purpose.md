# 02 — Purpose

---

# Overview

The Asset Business Object exists to provide a single, governed representation of every enterprise asset throughout its lifecycle.

Assets are critical organisational resources that enable operations, service delivery, production and strategic growth.

The Asset Business Object standardises asset governance, ownership, maintenance, utilisation and retirement while ensuring consistency across departments, business units and technology platforms.

---

# Why the Asset Business Object Exists

Organisations often manage thousands of assets distributed across multiple locations, departments and systems.

Without a governed Asset Business Object they commonly experience:

- fragmented asset records
- inconsistent ownership
- duplicate purchases
- inaccurate asset valuation
- poor maintenance planning
- unplanned equipment failures
- compliance risks
- limited lifecycle visibility
- ineffective capital planning

The Asset Business Object addresses these challenges by providing one canonical enterprise model for asset management.

# Business Problems Solved

## Asset Visibility

Organisations lack a consolidated view of enterprise assets.

Swissbay provides one governed Asset register.

---

## Ownership

Asset accountability is often unclear.

Swissbay assigns defined Business Owners and Custodians to every asset.

---

## Maintenance

Maintenance is frequently reactive.

Swissbay supports planned, preventive and predictive maintenance.

---

## Financial Control

Asset valuations and depreciation are often inconsistent.

Swissbay provides governed lifecycle and financial information.

---

## Compliance

Asset inspections and certifications may be missed.

Swissbay enables scheduled compliance monitoring and auditability.

---

## Utilisation

Assets are often underutilised or overused.

Swissbay provides utilisation tracking and optimisation.

- ownership
- classification

- location
