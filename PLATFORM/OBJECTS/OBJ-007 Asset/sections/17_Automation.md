# 17 — Automation

---

# Overview

The Automation Model defines how the Asset Business Object participates in automated business processes within Swissbay Nexus.

Automation coordinates asset registration, lifecycle transitions, maintenance scheduling, inspections, transfers, integrations and Artificial Intelligence by responding to business events throughout the Asset Lifecycle.

Automation improves operational consistency while preserving governance and human accountability.

---

# Purpose

The Asset Automation Model exists to:

- automate asset administration
- improve governance
- reduce manual effort
- strengthen compliance
- improve reporting
- support Artificial Intelligence
- enable enterprise integration
- maximise operational efficiency

Automation supports enterprise operations rather than replacing them.

---

# Automation Principles

Asset automation follows these principles:

- business-driven
- event-driven
- governed
- explainable
- auditable
- secure
- reversible where practical

Every automation must deliver measurable business value.
