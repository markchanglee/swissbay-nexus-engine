# 11 — Department Usage

---

# Overview

The Asset Business Object is an enterprise capability that supports nearly every department within the organisation.

While Asset Management governs the overall lifecycle of enterprise assets, multiple business units contribute to acquisition, operation, maintenance, finance, compliance and retirement.

This document defines departmental responsibilities and governance boundaries for enterprise asset management.

---

# Purpose

The Department Usage Model exists to:

- define departmental responsibilities
- strengthen governance
- improve collaboration
- eliminate ownership ambiguity
- improve accountability
- support Artificial Intelligence
- enable enterprise-wide asset management

Assets are shared enterprise resources governed through clearly defined responsibilities.

---

# Department Participation

The following departments interact with the Asset Business Object:

- Executive Management
- Asset Management
- Operations
- Engineering
- Maintenance
- Finance
- Procurement
- Information Technology
- Information Security
- Facilities Management
- Legal
- Human Resources
- Artificial Intelligence

Each department performs governed activities throughout the Asset Lifecycle.

# Executive Management

## Responsibilities

Executive Management provides strategic oversight.

Activities include:

- approve capital investment
- review asset portfolio
- approve strategic replacements
- monitor enterprise utilisation
- review lifecycle performance

### Primary Responsibilities

- Strategic Investment
- Governance
- Capital Planning

# Collaboration Principles

Departments should:

- maintain accurate asset information
- communicate lifecycle changes
- report operational issues
- follow governed workflows
- protect enterprise assets
- support continuous improvement

Successful asset management requires enterprise collaboration.

---

# Department Governance

Department responsibilities require:

- defined ownership
- documented authority
- workflow participation
- audit logging
- role-based security

Governance remains explicit throughout the Asset Lifecycle.

# Department Usage Summary

The Department Usage Model defines how every organisational function contributes to enterprise asset management within Swissbay Nexus.

By assigning clear responsibilities across governance, operations, engineering, finance, procurement and maintenance, Swissbay enables coordinated lifecycle management while maintaining accountability, transparency and operational excellence.
