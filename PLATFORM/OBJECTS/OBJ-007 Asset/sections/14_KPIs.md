# 14 — Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

---

# Overview

The Asset KPI Model defines the official performance measurements used to evaluate enterprise assets throughout their lifecycle within Swissbay Nexus.

KPIs provide consistent, governed metrics for measuring operational performance, maintenance effectiveness, financial value and asset reliability.

Every KPI follows the Swissbay KPI Standard (KPI-000).

---

# Purpose

The Asset KPI Model exists to:

- measure asset performance
- improve operational reliability
- strengthen governance
- optimise maintenance
- improve investment planning
- support Artificial Intelligence
- maximise asset value

KPIs measure business outcomes rather than operational activity.

# KPI-ASSET-001 — Asset Health Score

## Purpose

Provides an overall assessment of asset condition.

### Inputs

- operational condition
- maintenance history
- inspection results
- utilisation
- failure history

### Frequency

Daily

### Audience

- Asset Management
- Engineering
- Executive

---

# KPI-ASSET-002 — Asset Availability

## Formula

```
Available Operating Time

÷

Scheduled Operating Time

×

100
```

Measures the percentage of time an asset is available for use.

---

# KPI-ASSET-003 — Asset Utilisation

## Formula

```
Actual Operating Hours

÷

Available Operating Hours

×

100
```

Measures how effectively the asset is being utilised.

---

# KPI-ASSET-004 — Maintenance Compliance

## Formula

```
Completed Planned Maintenance

÷

Scheduled Maintenance

×

100
```

Measures adherence to maintenance plans.

---

# KPI-ASSET-005 — Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)

## Formula

```
Total Operating Time

÷

Number of Failures
```

Higher values indicate greater reliability.

---

# KPI-ASSET-006 — Mean Time To Repair (MTTR)

## Formula

```
Total Repair Time

÷

Number of Repairs
```

Lower values indicate faster recovery.

---

# KPI-ASSET-007 — Remaining Useful Life Accuracy

Measures the accuracy of AI predictions compared to actual asset performance over time.

Supports continuous AI model improvement.

---

# KPI-ASSET-008 — Inspection Compliance

## Formula

```
Completed Inspections

÷

Scheduled Inspections

×

100
```

Measures regulatory and operational compliance.

---

# KPI-ASSET-009 — Asset Lifecycle Cost

Measures the total cost of ownership including:

- acquisition
- maintenance
- upgrades
- operation
- disposal

Supports investment planning.

---

# KPI-ASSET-010 — Replacement Forecast Accuracy

Measures how accurately replacement planning aligns with actual asset retirement and performance.

Supports capital budgeting.

# KPI Validation

Before approval verify:

- KPI purpose documented
- calculation defined
- owner assigned
- reporting frequency defined
- measurable business value demonstrated

# KPI Summary

The Asset KPI Portfolio provides the official measurements for evaluating enterprise assets within Swissbay Nexus.

By defining consistent, governed and measurable indicators, Swissbay enables organisations to optimise reliability, maximise utilisation, improve maintenance performance and strengthen strategic asset management.
