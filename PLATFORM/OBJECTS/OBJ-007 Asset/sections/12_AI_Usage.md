# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence enhances the Asset Business Object by providing predictive analytics, intelligent recommendations, anomaly detection and lifecycle optimisation throughout the asset lifecycle.

AI supports Asset Managers, Engineers, Maintenance Teams, Finance and Executives by transforming governed asset information into actionable insights.

AI never replaces engineering judgement, operational responsibility or financial governance.

All AI capabilities operate in accordance with the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000).

---

# Purpose

The AI Usage Model exists to:

- improve asset reliability
- optimise maintenance
- reduce lifecycle costs
- improve utilisation
- strengthen governance
- support executive decision-making
- improve sustainability
- enable intelligent asset management

AI augments people rather than replacing them.

---

# AI Principles

Artificial Intelligence within Asset Management follows these principles:

- explainable
- governed
- transparent
- auditable
- secure
- human supervised
- business-first

Recommendations must always include supporting evidence.

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI supports enterprise asset management by:

- predicting equipment failure
- forecasting Remaining Useful Life
- analysing utilisation
- detecting anomalies
- recommending maintenance
- forecasting replacement timing
- summarising asset performance

AI assists decision-making while preserving accountability.

---

# AI Capabilities

## Predictive Maintenance

AI analyses:

- maintenance history
- inspection results
- failure patterns
- operating conditions
- sensor information (where available)

Outputs include maintenance recommendations and probability of failure.

---

## Remaining Useful Life Forecasting

AI estimates:

- remaining operational lifespan
- replacement timing
- lifecycle confidence
- degradation trends

Supports long-term capital planning.

---

## Utilisation Analysis

AI identifies:

- underutilised assets
- overutilised assets
- idle equipment
- bottlenecks
- redeployment opportunities

Improves operational efficiency.

---

## Failure Prediction

AI evaluates:

- historical failures
- operating conditions
- inspection trends
- maintenance frequency
- environmental factors

Outputs include:

- failure probability
- confidence score
- recommended intervention

---

## Asset Health Analysis

AI continuously evaluates:

- operational condition
- maintenance history
- inspection compliance
- lifecycle stage
- utilisation trends

Produces an overall Asset Health Score.

---

## Executive Summaries

AI automatically prepares:

- asset portfolio summaries
- maintenance performance
- utilisation trends
- replacement forecasts
- operational risks

Summaries support governance meetings and executive reporting.

---

# AI Inputs

Artificial Intelligence consumes:

- Asset Status
- Maintenance History
- Inspection Records
- Financial Information
- Utilisation Metrics
- Lifecycle Information
- Ownership
- Relationships
- Audit History

Only validated and governed information may be consumed.

---

# AI Outputs

AI may generate:

- maintenance recommendations
- replacement recommendations
- health scores
- lifecycle forecasts
- anomaly alerts
- utilisation reports
- executive summaries

Outputs remain linked to supporting evidence.

---

# Human Oversight

The following decisions remain exclusively human:

- asset acquisition
- asset disposal
- financial valuation
- capital approval
- governance approval
- policy exceptions

AI recommendations are advisory only.

---

# Explainability

Every AI recommendation should include:

- recommendation
- evidence
- confidence score
- contributing factors
- recommended actions

Users should understand why AI reached each conclusion.

---

# Governance

AI usage requires:

- approved models
- governed datasets
- audit logging
- security enforcement
- human accountability

AI activity must remain fully traceable.

---

# AI Summary

The AI Usage Model transforms the Asset Business Object into an intelligent enterprise capability.

By combining governed asset information with predictive analytics, explainable recommendations and lifecycle optimisation, Swissbay Nexus improves operational reliability, reduces maintenance costs and supports strategic asset management while preserving governance and human accountability.
