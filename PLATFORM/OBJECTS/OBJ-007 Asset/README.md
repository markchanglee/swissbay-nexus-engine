# OBJ-007 — Asset

## Overview

The Asset Business Object defines the governed representation of every physical, digital and intangible asset managed within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Assets represent resources that provide value to the organisation throughout their lifecycle. They may be acquired, constructed, leased, maintained, transferred, depreciated and ultimately disposed of.

The Asset Business Object provides a single source of truth for asset identity, ownership, classification, lifecycle, valuation, location, maintenance and governance.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Asset Business Object is to establish one canonical representation of enterprise assets across Swissbay Nexus.

Assets may include:

- Buildings
- Mining Equipment
- Vehicles
- Machinery
- Computers
- Servers
- Network Equipment
- Software Licences
- Production Equipment
- Laboratory Equipment
- Office Equipment
- Infrastructure
- Land
- Intellectual Property

Every Asset follows the same governed lifecycle regardless of department or business unit.
