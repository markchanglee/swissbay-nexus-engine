# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Customer Business Object is designed to evolve alongside Swissbay.

This roadmap provides a structured view of planned enhancements, ensuring that future development remains aligned with the Swissbay Nexus vision while maintaining backwards compatibility wherever practical.

The roadmap is a planning document.

Features listed within this document represent intended capabilities rather than guaranteed delivery commitments.

Priorities may change based on business strategy, customer feedback and technological advancement.

---

# Roadmap Principles

The Customer Business Object should evolve according to the following principles.

## Principle 1 — Business Driven

Business requirements drive development.

Technology enables business rather than defining it.

---

## Principle 2 — Backwards Compatibility

Existing implementations should continue functioning whenever practical.

Breaking changes should only occur when they deliver significant long-term value.

---

## Principle 3 — AI First

Every new capability should consider how Artificial Intelligence can improve user productivity while maintaining governance.

---

## Principle 4 — Modular Growth

New capabilities should extend the Customer Business Object rather than replacing it.

The object should become richer over time without increasing unnecessary complexity.

# Success Criteria

The Customer Business Object roadmap is considered successful when:

- Customer information remains the single source of truth.
- AI improves employee productivity.
- Business processes become increasingly automated.
- Customer relationships become measurable and predictable.
- New capabilities can be introduced without redesigning the object.
- The object scales across multiple companies and industries.

---

# Roadmap Governance

All roadmap items should:

- align with the Swissbay Nexus Constitution
- support the Business Specification Standard
- comply with AI governance
- maintain security principles
- preserve data quality
- be reviewed before implementation

Roadmap changes require approval through the Nexus governance process.

---

# Roadmap Summary

The Customer Business Object has been designed as a long-term strategic asset rather than a static software component.

Its evolution will be guided by business value, governance, Artificial Intelligence and operational excellence.

By following this roadmap, Swissbay ensures that the Customer object continues to grow alongside the organisation while remaining stable, reusable and future-ready.

The roadmap provides a clear direction for developers, business analysts, architects and AI Agents, ensuring that every enhancement contributes to a consistent and intelligent business operating platform.
