# 02 — Purpose

## Purpose Statement

The purpose of the Customer Business Object is to establish a single, authoritative definition of what a Customer means within the Swissbay ecosystem.

Every department, workflow, report, dashboard, integration, and AI Agent must reference this object whenever customer-related information is required. By defining the Customer once and reusing that definition everywhere, Swissbay creates consistency across the organisation and eliminates conflicting interpretations.

The Customer object is not merely a database record or CRM entry. It represents an active commercial relationship that generates value for both Swissbay and the organisation it serves.

---

# Why This Object Exists

As businesses grow, customer information naturally becomes fragmented.

Sales teams maintain contact lists.

Finance maintains debtor records.

Operations track delivery addresses.

Marketing manages campaign audiences.

Procurement monitors purchasing trends.

Customer Success records support interactions.

When each department develops its own understanding of a customer, inconsistencies begin to appear.

Examples include:

* Different customer names across systems.
* Multiple versions of the same contact.
* Conflicting payment terms.
* Duplicate customer records.
* Incorrect reporting.
* AI producing inconsistent recommendations because it is using different sources of truth.

The Customer Business Object exists to eliminate these problems by providing one governed definition that every part of the organisation uses.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objective of this object is to ensure that Swissbay always knows:

* Who the customer is.
* What relationship exists with the customer.
* Who owns that relationship.
* What products or services the customer purchases.
* How valuable the customer is to the business.
* What commitments exist between Swissbay and the customer.
* What actions should happen next.

This object provides the foundation for every customer-facing activity performed by Swissbay.

---

# Strategic Purpose

From a strategic perspective, the Customer object enables Swissbay to build long-term commercial relationships rather than isolated transactions.

Instead of asking:

> "How do we complete this sale?"

Swissbay should ask:

> "How do we grow this relationship over the next five years?"

This shift changes the focus from short-term revenue to long-term customer value.

---

# Operational Purpose

Operationally, the Customer object creates a consistent reference point for every department.

Sales uses it to manage opportunities and quotations.

Operations uses it to fulfil orders accurately.

Finance uses it to invoice customers and manage credit.

Marketing uses it to segment audiences.

Customer Success uses it to improve retention.

Management uses it to understand business performance.

Although every department interacts with the Customer differently, they all rely on the same underlying business object.

---

# Purpose Within Swissbay Nexus

Within the Nexus platform, the Customer object serves as the central node that connects multiple business objects together.

Examples include:

* Leads
* Opportunities
* Quotes
* Sales Orders
* Invoices
* Meetings
* Tasks
* Contracts
* Products Purchased
* Complaints
* AI Insights

Rather than duplicating information, these objects establish relationships with the Customer object, allowing Swissbay to maintain a single source of truth while enabling rich business intelligence.

---

# Purpose for Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence depends on structured, trustworthy information.

The Customer object provides that foundation.

Approved AI Agents may use Customer information to:

* summarise account history
* identify purchasing patterns
* recommend follow-up activities
* highlight business risks
* identify upselling opportunities
* prepare meeting briefs
* generate executive summaries

However, AI must never invent customer information or infer business facts that are not supported by approved data.

The Customer object therefore provides both the context and the governance required for responsible AI-assisted decision making.

---

# Success Criteria

The purpose of this object is considered successful when:

* Every department uses the same customer definition.
* Duplicate customer records are eliminated.
* Customer information is trusted across the business.
* AI recommendations are based on approved customer data.
* Reports consistently produce the same customer metrics.
* Customer relationships become measurable and manageable over time.
* New employees can understand how Swissbay manages customers by reading this specification alone.

---

# Design Principles

This object has been designed according to the following principles:

1. **Single Source of Truth** – One official definition of every customer.
2. **Business Before Technology** – The business relationship is more important than the software storing it.
3. **Relationship-Centric** – Customers are long-term partners, not isolated transactions.
4. **Reusable** – The same object supports Sales, Finance, Operations, Procurement, Marketing, Customer Success and AI.
5. **Governed** – Changes to customer definitions follow Nexus governance standards.
6. **AI-Ready** – The object is structured so AI can assist without introducing ambiguity.
7. **Scalable** – The model should support Swissbay as it expands into new industries, products and companies.

---

# Conclusion

The Customer Business Object is the foundation of Swissbay's commercial operating model.

It exists to ensure that every interaction with a customer is built upon a shared understanding of who that customer is, why the relationship exists, and how value is created over time.

By governing customer information through a single Business Object, Swissbay establishes a consistent, scalable and AI-ready operating model that supports sustainable growth across the organisation.
