# 14 — Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

---

# Overview

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) measure the effectiveness of the Customer Business Object.

The objective of these KPIs is to provide objective, measurable indicators of customer growth, customer health, commercial performance and operational excellence.

KPIs should drive business decisions.

Every KPI should answer one or more business questions.

If a KPI does not influence behaviour or decision-making, it should be reconsidered.

Customer KPIs support:

- Executive Management
- Sales
- Finance
- Operations
- Procurement
- Customer Success
- Artificial Intelligence

---

# KPI Design Principles

Swissbay follows the following KPI principles.

## Principle 1 — Business First

KPIs measure business outcomes rather than system activity.

---

## Principle 2 — Objective Measurement

Every KPI should have a clear calculation method.

Results should not depend on interpretation.

---

## Principle 3 — Actionable

Each KPI should lead to a business action.

If no action is possible, the KPI has limited value.

---

## Principle 4 — Governed

KPIs must use governed Business Objects as their source.

Manual calculations should be avoided.

---

## Principle 5 — Historical

KPIs should support trend analysis over time.

Point-in-time values are useful, but trends reveal business performance.

# Customer Growth KPIs

## KPI-CUST-001

### Total Customers

**Purpose**

Measure the total number of governed Customer records.

**Formula**

```
Count(Customer)
```

**Owner**

Sales

**Target**

Growing steadily.

---

## KPI-CUST-002

### New Customers

**Purpose**

Measure customer acquisition.

**Formula**

```
Customers Created
during selected period
```

---

## KPI-CUST-003

### Customer Growth Rate

**Formula**

```
(New Customers ÷
Opening Customers)
× 100
```

---

# Revenue KPIs

## KPI-CUST-004

### Revenue per Customer

**Formula**

```
Total Revenue
÷
Active Customers
```

---

## KPI-CUST-005

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

**Purpose**

Estimate the total expected value generated by a Customer over the relationship.

Factors include:

- revenue
- profitability
- retention
- purchase frequency

---

## KPI-CUST-006

### Average Order Value

**Formula**

```
Revenue
÷
Number of Orders
```

---

## KPI-CUST-007

### Revenue Concentration

**Purpose**

Identify dependence on a small number of Customers.

Example

```
Top 10 Customers

÷

Total Revenue
```

Every KPI should define:

- Current Value
- Target Value
- Warning Threshold
- Critical Threshold
- Previous Period
- Trend
- Owner
- Last Updated

This enables proactive management rather than reactive reporting.

# KPI Summary

KPIs provide the measurable definition of Customer success within Swissbay Nexus.

They enable every department to evaluate performance using governed, consistent metrics while supporting executive decision-making, operational excellence and continuous improvement.

Together with Dashboards, Business Rules and AI, these KPIs transform the Customer Business Object into a living performance management system that continuously measures and improves the commercial health of the organisation.
