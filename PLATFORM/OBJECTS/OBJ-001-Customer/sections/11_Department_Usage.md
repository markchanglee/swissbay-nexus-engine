# 11 — Department Usage

---

# Overview

The Customer Business Object is the only Business Object shared by every operational department within Swissbay.

Although each department interacts with the Customer from a different perspective, they all rely on the same governed Customer record.

This ensures every decision is based on consistent, trusted information.

Departments should never create separate customer records or maintain conflicting customer information.

Instead, each department contributes specialised knowledge while relying on the Customer Business Object as the single source of truth.

---

# Department Usage Philosophy

Swissbay believes that customer ownership is shared operationally but governed centrally.

Every department has responsibilities.

Every department creates value.

Every department contributes knowledge.

No department owns the Customer exclusively.

The Customer belongs to the business.

---

- Meeting Notes

## Key Decisions

- follow-up actions

# Shared Responsibilities

Every department shares responsibility for maintaining accurate customer information.

Departments should:

- capture information promptly
- avoid duplication
- follow Business Rules
- respect ownership boundaries
- contribute high-quality information
- maintain auditability

---

# Department Collaboration

The Customer object enables collaboration between departments.

Example:

```
Sales wins Opportunity

↓

Finance approves Payment Terms

↓

Procurement prepares sourcing

↓

Operations delivers products

↓

Customer Success monitors satisfaction

↓

Management reviews performance

↓

AI analyses relationship
```

Each department performs different work while using the same Customer record.

---

# Governance

Department responsibilities do not override Business Rules.

Where conflicts occur:

Business Rules take precedence.

If ownership is unclear:

The Nexus Governance process must be followed.

---

# Success Measures

Effective departmental use of the Customer object should result in:

- improved collaboration

- higher customer satisfaction
- faster response times

---

# Department Usage Summary

The Customer Business Object provides every department with a shared understanding of the customer relationship.

Each department contributes specialised knowledge while relying on the same governed business object.

This approach allows Swissbay to operate as one coordinated organisation rather than a collection of disconnected departments.

The result is improved collaboration, stronger governance, higher data quality and better business decisions across the enterprise.
