# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is an integral capability within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

The purpose of AI is to enhance decision-making, reduce repetitive work, improve customer relationships and surface business insights that would otherwise remain hidden.

AI exists to assist people—not replace them.

Within the Customer Business Object, AI consumes governed Customer information to generate recommendations, summaries, predictions and operational intelligence.

The Customer Business Object remains the authoritative source of customer information.

AI may analyse Customer data.

AI may recommend actions.

AI may prepare drafts.

AI may never become the owner of Customer information.

---

# AI Philosophy

Swissbay believes that Artificial Intelligence should amplify human capability.

AI should make employees:

- faster
- more informed
- more consistent
- more proactive

AI should never remove business accountability.

Final ownership always remains with authorised employees.

---

# AI Principles

All AI operating on Customer information must follow these principles.

## Principle 1 — Customer Data is Governed

AI only consumes approved Customer information.

AI must never create unofficial Customer records.

---

## Principle 2 — AI Assists

AI provides recommendations.

Humans make decisions.

---

## Principle 3 — Explainability

AI recommendations should explain:

- why the recommendation was made
- what information was used
- confidence level
- assumptions made

Users should understand how AI reached its conclusions.

---

## Principle 4 — Transparency

Users must always know when information has been generated by AI.

AI-generated content should be clearly identified.

---

## Principle 5 — Trustworthy Data

AI recommendations are only as reliable as the Customer information available.

Where information is incomplete, AI should highlight missing data rather than making assumptions.

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI supports the Customer object in five primary ways.

## 1. Information Retrieval

AI rapidly retrieves customer information.

Examples include:

- customer summaries
- recent activity
- quotation history
- outstanding invoices
- purchasing behaviour
- meeting history
- complaints

---

## 2. Business Analysis

AI identifies patterns including:

- purchasing trends
- declining activity
- seasonal demand
- overdue payments
- growth opportunities
- customer concentration

---

## 3. Recommendations

AI recommends:

- follow-up actions
- cross-selling opportunities
- upselling opportunities
- meeting priorities
- dormant customer recovery
- relationship improvements

Recommendations remain advisory.

---

## 4. Content Generation

AI may generate:

- meeting agendas
- account summaries
- quotation drafts
- executive briefings
- customer review reports
- follow-up emails
- proposal outlines

Generated content requires human review before external use.

---

## 5. Continuous Monitoring

AI continuously monitors customer health.

Examples include:

- inactivity
- churn indicators
- complaint frequency
- payment deterioration
- declining order frequency
- strategic opportunities

# AI Restrictions

AI must never:

- create fake Customer information
- invent business facts
- modify governed Customer data directly
- approve commercial decisions
- approve contracts
- approve pricing
- approve payment terms
- approve credit limits
- archive Customers
- delete Customer information

These actions require authorised human approval.

# AI Confidence Levels

Swissbay recommends AI confidence levels.

| Confidence | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| High | Strong evidence available |
| Medium | Reasonable evidence with some uncertainty |
| Low | Insufficient information |
| Unknown | AI unable to determine outcome |

Low-confidence recommendations should automatically request human review.

---

# AI Learning

Swissbay AI improves through:

- approved historical data
- user feedback
- completed workflows
- successful customer outcomes
- governance reviews

AI should never learn from unapproved or inaccurate information.

---

- Customer Business Rules
- Customer Validation Rules

AI behaviour must remain fully auditable.

# AI Usage Summary

Artificial Intelligence is a strategic capability within Swissbay Nexus.

Its purpose is to support employees with trusted insights, intelligent recommendations and operational efficiency while preserving human accountability.

By clearly defining AI responsibilities, permissions and limitations, Swissbay ensures that AI strengthens customer relationships without compromising governance, transparency or business control.

The Customer Business Object provides the trusted foundation upon which every AI interaction is built.
