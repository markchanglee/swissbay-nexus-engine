# 09 — Business Rules

---

# Overview

Business Rules define the policies, constraints and behaviours that govern the Customer Business Object.

These rules ensure that Customer information remains accurate, trustworthy and consistent across Swissbay Nexus.

Unlike workflows, which describe *how* activities are performed, Business Rules define *what is permitted* and *what is prohibited*.

Every employee, application, workflow, integration and AI Agent interacting with the Customer object must comply with these rules.

---

# Business Rule Philosophy

Swissbay believes that data quality is achieved through governance rather than correction.

Business Rules exist to:

- protect data integrity
- prevent inconsistent behaviour
- reduce operational risk
- improve reporting accuracy
- support regulatory compliance
- enable reliable automation
- provide trustworthy AI recommendations

Business Rules apply equally to manual processes and automated systems.

---

# Rule Categories

Customer Business Rules are grouped into the following categories:

- Identity Rules
- Ownership Rules
- Relationship Rules
- Financial Rules
- Operational Rules
- Lifecycle Rules
- Data Quality Rules
- Security Rules
- AI Rules
- Governance Rules

---

# Identity Rules

## BR-CUST-001

Every Customer must have a unique Customer ID.

Customer IDs are generated by the system and may never be reused.

---

## BR-CUST-002

A Customer may not exist without a Legal Name.

Trading Names may change.

Legal Names identify the official business entity.

---

## BR-CUST-003

A Customer Status must always be assigned.

Only approved lifecycle statuses may be used.

---

## BR-CUST-004

Customer records may never be duplicated.

If duplicates are discovered they must be merged according to Swissbay governance procedures.

---

# Ownership Rules

## BR-CUST-005

Every Customer must have an Account Owner.

The Account Owner is responsible for the commercial relationship.

---

## BR-CUST-006

Only authorised users may change the Account Owner.

Ownership changes must be recorded in the audit history.

---

## BR-CUST-007

Departments may contribute information to the Customer record but ownership remains governed.

---

# Relationship Rules

## BR-CUST-008

Every Quote must reference a valid Customer or an approved Opportunity.

---

## BR-CUST-009

Every Sales Order must reference one Customer.

---

## BR-CUST-010

Every Invoice must reference one Customer.

Invoices may never exist independently.

---

## BR-CUST-011

Customer relationships must use Business Object IDs rather than free-text references.

Example:

```
CUSTOMER-000421
```

not

```
ABC Mining
```

---

# Financial Rules

## BR-CUST-012

Payment Terms must be selected from the approved Payment Terms list.

---

## BR-CUST-013

Credit Limits may only be approved by Finance or delegated authority.

---

## BR-CUST-014

Customers with overdue accounts may require approval before additional credit sales.

---

## BR-CUST-015

Financial information must be maintained by Finance.

Sales may recommend changes but may not approve them.

---

# Operational Rules

## BR-CUST-016

An active Customer must have at least one valid delivery location before fulfilment begins.

---

## BR-CUST-017

Operational delivery constraints must be captured where applicable.

Examples include:

- site access
- delivery hours
- PPE requirements
- induction requirements
- security procedures

---

## BR-CUST-018

Customer operational information must remain current.

Outdated information creates delivery risk.

---

# Lifecycle Rules

## BR-CUST-019

A Lead may only become a Customer after satisfying the approved qualification criteria.

---

## BR-CUST-020

A Customer may not be archived while open Orders, Quotes or Invoices exist.

---

## BR-CUST-021

Dormant Customers remain Customers until formally archived.

---

## BR-CUST-022

Archived Customers retain their complete commercial history.

Historical information must never be deleted.

---

# Data Quality Rules

## BR-CUST-023

Required fields must be completed before Customer activation.

---

## BR-CUST-024

Customer information should be reviewed regularly.

Review frequency should be determined by Customer value and activity.

---

## BR-CUST-025

Structured fields should always be preferred over free-text notes.

---

## BR-CUST-026

Changes to key customer information must be auditable.

Examples include:

- Legal Name
- Customer Status
- Payment Terms
- Credit Limit
- Account Owner

---

# Security Rules

## BR-CUST-027

Customer information must only be accessible to authorised users.

---

## BR-CUST-028

Sensitive financial information must follow role-based access controls.

---

## BR-CUST-029

Commercial documents must remain linked to the Customer throughout their retention period.

---

# AI Rules

## BR-CUST-030

AI Agents may analyse Customer information but may not modify governed fields directly.

---

## BR-CUST-031

AI recommendations remain advisory.

Final business decisions remain the responsibility of authorised employees.

---

## BR-CUST-032

AI must never invent Customer information.

Where required information is unavailable, AI must explicitly identify the missing information.

---

## BR-CUST-033

AI outputs relating to Customers should identify the data sources used wherever possible.

---

# Governance Rules

## BR-CUST-034

All Customer changes must comply with:

- NX-000 Constitution
- BS-000 Business Specification Standard
- DATA-000 Data Standard
- AI-000 AI Operating Standard
- OBJ-000 Business Object Standard

---

## BR-CUST-035

Business Rules may only be changed through the Nexus governance process.

Individual departments may not redefine Customer rules independently.

---

# Exception Handling

Where exceptional circumstances require deviation from these Business Rules:

- the exception must be documented
- the approving authority must be identified
- the business justification must be recorded
- the duration of the exception should be defined
- the exception must be auditable

Exceptions are intended to support legitimate business needs rather than bypass governance.

---

# Compliance

Compliance with these Business Rules should be monitored through:

- periodic data quality reviews
- internal audits
- dashboard reporting
- validation rules
- workflow controls
- AI monitoring
- executive governance reviews

Non-compliance should trigger corrective action.

# Business Rules Summary

Business Rules transform the Customer Business Object from a passive data record into a governed business asset.

They ensure that Customer information is created, maintained and used consistently across Swissbay.

By defining clear rules for identity, ownership, finance, operations, lifecycle, security and AI, Swissbay establishes a trusted operating model that supports automation, analytics and long-term business growth.

These rules are mandatory for all users, systems and AI Agents operating within the Swissbay Nexus platform.
