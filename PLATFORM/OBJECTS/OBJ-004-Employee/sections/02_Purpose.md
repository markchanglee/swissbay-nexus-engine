# 01 — Executive Summary

---

# Overview

The Employee Business Object defines the governed representation of every person authorised to perform work within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Employees form the organisational foundation of Swissbay by providing identity, accountability, ownership and operational responsibility across all business processes.

Unlike transactional Business Objects, the Employee is a Master Business Object that represents a long-lived organisational entity. It serves as the authoritative source of workforce information used throughout workflows, approvals, security, reporting, automation and Artificial Intelligence.

Every action performed within Swissbay Nexus is ultimately attributable to an Employee Business Object.

---

# Business Purpose

The Employee Business Object establishes a single, governed representation of every employee, contractor, consultant or authorised worker across the organisation.

It standardises workforce information including:

- identity
- employment status
- organisational hierarchy
- department
- manager relationships
- job roles
- business responsibilities
- system permissions

This ensures that every Business Object references a consistent and trusted representation of the people responsible for enterprise operations.

---

# Business Importance

Employees are fundamental to enterprise governance.

Every major business capability depends on the Employee Business Object, including:

- workflow approvals
- task assignments
- audit history
- security permissions
- business ownership
- reporting structures
- operational accountability
- AI authorisation

Without a governed Employee Business Object, organisations experience inconsistent identities, duplicated user records, unclear ownership and fragmented security models.
