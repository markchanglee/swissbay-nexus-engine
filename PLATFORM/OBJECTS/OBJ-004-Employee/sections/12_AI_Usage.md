# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is a governed participant within the Employee Business Object.

Rather than replacing Human Resources, Managers or Executive Management, AI enhances workforce decision-making by analysing organisational data, identifying trends, forecasting workforce requirements and recommending actions.

Every AI capability operates within the governance boundaries defined by the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000), Security Standard (SEC-000) and Governance Framework.

Artificial Intelligence supports people.

It never replaces accountable decision-makers.

---

# Purpose

The AI Usage Model exists to:

- improve workforce planning
- improve organisational visibility
- improve employee development
- optimise resource allocation
- support succession planning
- improve recruitment planning
- improve organisational effectiveness
- support executive decision-making

AI exists to augment Human Resources and management while preserving governance and accountability.

# Explainability

Every significant AI recommendation should include:

- recommendation
- confidence score
- supporting Employees
- supporting Business Rules
- supporting KPIs
- assumptions
- alternative recommendations

Explainability is mandatory.

---

# Confidence Levels

Swissbay classifies AI confidence as:

| Confidence | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| 95–100% | Very High |
| 85–94% | High |
| 70–84% | Moderate |
| Below 70% | Low |

Low-confidence recommendations require additional human review.

---

# AI Restrictions

Artificial Intelligence may not:

- hire Employees
- terminate Employees
- promote Employees
- reduce salaries
- assign security permissions
- modify reporting structures
- override Business Rules
- bypass governance

Employment accountability always remains with authorised people.

---

# AI Security

Artificial Intelligence must:

- inherit user permissions
- respect Employee classifications
- protect personal information
- maintain audit history
- explain recommendations

AI security follows SEC-000.

---

# AI Governance

Every AI capability requires:

- Business Owner
- Technical Owner
- Training Data Source
- Review Frequency
- Performance Monitoring
- Governance Approval

AI capabilities are governed enterprise assets.

# AI Usage Summary

The Employee Business Object enables Artificial Intelligence to become an intelligent workforce advisor within Swissbay Nexus.

By combining trusted workforce information, governed Business Rules, explainable recommendations and continuous monitoring, Swissbay Nexus delivers AI-assisted workforce management that improves organisational planning, employee development, resource allocation and executive decision-making while preserving governance, security and human accountability.
