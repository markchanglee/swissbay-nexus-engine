# 17 — Automation

---

# Overview

The Automation Model defines how the Employee Business Object participates in automated business processes throughout the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Automation improves workforce management by responding to business events, enforcing Business Rules, coordinating workflows and integrating enterprise systems.

Automation reduces manual effort while preserving governance, accountability and human oversight.

Automation supports people.

It never replaces accountable decision-makers.

---

# Purpose

The Employee Automation Model exists to:

- automate workforce administration
- improve onboarding
- simplify identity provisioning
- strengthen governance
- improve compliance
- reduce operational effort
- enable Artificial Intelligence
- improve enterprise integration

Every automation must provide measurable business value.

---

# Automation Principles

Employee automation follows these principles:

- business-driven
- event-driven
- governed
- explainable
- auditable
- secure
- reversible where practical

Automation simplifies business operations without replacing human accountability.

# AUTO-EMP-001 — Employee Record Creation

## Trigger

Offer Accepted

## Automation

Automatically:

- create Employee record
- generate Employee Identifier
- assign default lifecycle state
- notify Human Resources

## Outcome

Employee enters the Pre-Onboarding stage.

---

# AUTO-EMP-002 — Employee Onboarding

## Trigger

Employment Start Date

## Automation

Automatically:

- create onboarding checklist
- schedule induction
- notify Manager
- notify Human Resources
- generate onboarding tasks
- update dashboards

Human Resources remains accountable for onboarding completion.

---

# AUTO-EMP-003 — Identity Provisioning

## Trigger

Employee Activated

## Automation

Automatically:

- create enterprise account
- provision corporate email
- assign default security groups
- enable Multi-Factor Authentication
- create collaboration accounts
- register employee in identity services

Authentication remains governed by enterprise identity providers.

---

# AUTO-EMP-004 — Role & Permission Assignment

## Trigger

Business Role Assigned

## Automation

Automatically:

- assign application roles
- request approval where required
- validate segregation of duties
- notify Information Security
- update audit history

Approval authority always remains governed.

# Automation Summary

The Employee Automation Model transforms workforce administration into an intelligent, event-driven capability within Swissbay Nexus.

By combining governed workflows, Business Rules, Artificial Intelligence and enterprise integrations, automation reduces manual effort while preserving accountability, transparency and organisational control.

Automation enables Human Resources, Information Technology, Information Security and Executive Management to focus on strategic workforce activities while routine operational processes are executed consistently, securely and efficiently by the Swissbay Nexus platform.
