# 13 — Dashboards

---

# Overview

The Employee Business Object provides trusted workforce information for operational, strategic and executive dashboards throughout the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Dashboards transform governed Employee information into actionable business intelligence that supports workforce management, organisational planning, compliance, security, performance management and Artificial Intelligence.

Dashboards consume Employee information.

They never own or modify Employee data.

The Employee Business Object remains the authoritative source of workforce identity.

---

# Purpose

Employee dashboards exist to:

- monitor workforce health
- improve organisational visibility
- support workforce planning
- strengthen governance
- improve compliance
- monitor security
- enable AI-assisted decision making

Every dashboard should answer meaningful business questions.

# Dashboard Filters

All Employee dashboards should support filtering by:

- Employee
- Department
- Business Unit
- Manager
- Position
- Employment Type
- Employment Status
- Office Location
- Date Range

Filters should remain consistent across all dashboards.

---

# Drill-Down Navigation

Dashboards should support drill-down.

```text
Executive Workforce Dashboard

↓

Department Dashboard

↓

Manager Dashboard

↓

Employee Dashboard

↓

Employee Record

↓

Training

↓

Performance

↓

Audit History
```

Users should always be able to navigate back to the governing Employee Business Object.

# Dashboard Summary

The Employee Dashboard Portfolio transforms governed workforce information into enterprise intelligence.

By combining workforce visibility, organisational analytics, security monitoring, operational planning and AI-powered insights, Swissbay Nexus enables Human Resources, Information Technology, Information Security, Operations and Executive Management to make informed workforce decisions while maintaining complete traceability back to the Employee Business Object.

Dashboards are a decision-support capability built upon trusted workforce identity rather than independent reporting artefacts.
