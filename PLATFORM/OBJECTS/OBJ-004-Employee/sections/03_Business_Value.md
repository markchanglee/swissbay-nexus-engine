# 03 — Business Value

# Overview

The Employee Business Object provides the governed workforce foundation for the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Every employee represents an authorised organisational identity responsible for executing business processes, approving transactions, managing enterprise assets and maintaining operational accountability.

By establishing one enterprise-wide definition of an Employee, Swissbay enables consistent governance across Human Resources, Security, Operations, Finance, Artificial Intelligence and Enterprise Architecture.

The Employee Business Object transforms workforce information into a governed enterprise capability.

# Value Summary

The Employee Business Object is the organisational identity and accountability layer of Swissbay Nexus.

By providing a governed, reusable and trusted representation of every authorised worker, Swissbay creates a single source of truth connecting Human Resources, Security, Operations, Finance, Artificial Intelligence and Enterprise Governance.

The Employee Business Object enables secure enterprise operations, organisational transparency, intelligent workforce management and scalable business governance while ensuring that every enterprise action is attributable to an authorised individual.
