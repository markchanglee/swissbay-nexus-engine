# OBJ-004 — Employee

## Overview

The Employee Business Object defines the governed representation of every person who works for, represents, manages, supports or performs authorised work within Swissbay Nexus.

Employees are central to governance, workflows, approvals, security, accountability, audit history, AI permissions and operational execution.

The Employee Business Object provides a single source of truth for employee identity, role, department, responsibilities, reporting relationships and lifecycle status.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Employee Business Object is to establish one governed representation of employees across Swissbay Nexus.

This object ensures that every workflow, approval, task, audit record, dashboard, AI Agent and Business Object ownership model can reference the correct authorised person.
