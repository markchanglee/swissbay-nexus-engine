# 02 — Purpose

---

# Purpose Statement

The Sales Order Business Object exists to establish a single, governed representation of every customer sales transaction managed within Swissbay Nexus.

It captures the complete commercial agreement between the organisation and its customers, from the moment an order is placed through fulfilment, invoicing, delivery and completion.

The Sales Order Business Object serves as the authoritative source of truth for transactional sales information across Sales, Customer Service, Warehouse Operations, Finance, Executive Reporting and Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Business Problem

Without a governed Sales Order Business Object organisations commonly experience:

- duplicate customer orders
- inconsistent pricing
- fulfilment delays
- inventory allocation conflicts
- revenue discrepancies
- disconnected operational processes
- poor customer visibility
- unreliable reporting

When multiple systems independently manage sales transactions, commercial operations become fragmented and difficult to govern.

Swissbay Nexus eliminates these issues through a single governed Sales Order Business Object.
