# 01 — Executive Summary

---

# Overview

The Sales Order Business Object defines the governed representation of every commercial sales transaction processed within Swissbay Nexus.

A Sales Order captures the formal agreement between a Customer and the organisation for the purchase of one or more Products under agreed commercial terms.

Unlike master Business Objects such as Customer, Supplier and Product, a Sales Order represents a business event that progresses through a governed operational lifecycle from creation to fulfilment, invoicing and completion.

The Sales Order Business Object provides a single, trusted source of transactional truth across Sales, Finance, Warehousing, Customer Service and Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Business Purpose

The purpose of the Sales Order Business Object is to establish one governed definition of every customer order processed by the organisation.

It standardises how orders are created, approved, fulfilled, invoiced, delivered and completed across all channels including CRM, ERP, eCommerce and partner systems.

This ensures:

- one version of the truth
- consistent order processing
- accurate revenue reporting
- improved customer service
- trusted operational analytics
- AI-ready transaction data

---

# Business Importance

Sales Orders are the commercial heartbeat of the organisation.

Every completed sale passes through this Business Object.

It directly connects:

- Customers
- Products
- Warehouses
- Contracts
- Pricing
- Finance
- Inventory
- Customer Service

Without governed Sales Orders, organisations experience fulfilment delays, inconsistent revenue reporting, poor customer experiences and reduced operational visibility.
