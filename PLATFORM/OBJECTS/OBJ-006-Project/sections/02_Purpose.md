# 02 — Purpose

---

# Overview

The Project Business Object exists to provide a single, governed representation of every project undertaken within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Projects are the primary mechanism through which organisations execute strategy, deliver customer commitments, implement change and realise business value.

The Project Business Object standardises project governance, planning, execution, monitoring and closure, ensuring that every initiative follows a consistent enterprise model regardless of industry, department or delivery methodology.

---

# Why the Project Business Object Exists

Organisations execute hundreds or thousands of projects over time.

Without a governed Project Business Object they often experience:

- inconsistent project management
- duplicated work
- unclear ownership
- uncontrolled scope changes
- budget overruns
- schedule delays
- unmanaged risks
- inconsistent reporting
- poor executive visibility
- limited organisational learning

The Project Business Object addresses these challenges by providing one canonical enterprise model for project management.

---

# Enterprise Purpose

The Project Business Object enables organisations to:

- execute strategic initiatives

- govern project delivery

Projects become governed business assets rather than isolated operational activities.

# Business Problems Solved

The Project Business Object addresses common enterprise challenges including:

## Governance

Projects follow inconsistent approval and reporting processes.

Swissbay establishes one governed project lifecycle.

---

## Visibility

Executives lack a consolidated view of project health.

Swissbay provides enterprise-wide project reporting.

---

## Accountability

Project ownership is often unclear.

Swissbay assigns defined Business Owners, Project Managers and Sponsors to every project.

---

## Risk

Project risks are identified too late.

Swissbay enables continuous risk identification, monitoring and mitigation.

---

## Resource Management

Resources are frequently overallocated or underutilised.

Swissbay provides governed resource allocation and utilisation tracking.

---

## Knowledge Retention

Lessons learned are often lost after project completion.

Swissbay captures project knowledge for future reuse and continuous improvement.

# Purpose Summary

The Project Business Object establishes the enterprise execution framework within Swissbay Nexus.

By providing a governed, standardised representation of every project, Swissbay enables organisations to execute strategy with greater visibility, accountability, consistency and control.

The Project Business Object transforms projects from isolated initiatives into governed enterprise assets that deliver measurable business value while supporting automation, Artificial Intelligence and long-term organisational success.
