# 10 — Workflows

---

# Overview

The Workflow Model defines the governed business processes that manage Projects throughout their lifecycle.

Workflows coordinate approvals, planning, execution, monitoring and project closure while ensuring compliance with Business Rules, Security policies and governance standards.

Every workflow is event-driven and fully auditable.

---

# Purpose

The Project Workflow Model exists to:

- standardise project execution
- enforce governance
- automate approvals
- improve collaboration
- strengthen accountability
- improve reporting
- enable Artificial Intelligence

Workflows transform governance into repeatable business processes.

---

# Workflow Principles

Project workflows follow these principles:

- business-first
- event-driven
- governed
- auditable
- secure
- reusable
- explainable

Workflows describe business behaviour rather than system implementation.
