# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence enhances the Project Business Object by providing predictive analytics, intelligent recommendations, automated summarisation and decision support throughout the project lifecycle.

AI does not replace Project Managers, Sponsors or Governance Boards.

Instead, AI augments human decision-making by identifying patterns, forecasting outcomes and surfacing actionable insights from governed project information.

All AI interactions must comply with the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000).

---

# Purpose

The AI Usage Model exists to:

- improve project delivery
- support executive decision-making
- reduce project risk
- improve forecasting
- optimise resources
- identify trends
- strengthen governance
- improve organisational learning

AI provides recommendations while preserving human accountability.

---

# AI Principles

Artificial Intelligence within Projects follows these principles:

- explainable
- governed
- auditable
- transparent
- secure
- human supervised
- business-first

AI recommendations are advisory unless explicitly governed otherwise.

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI supports project teams by:

- analysing project health
- forecasting delivery dates
- estimating completion confidence
- identifying budget variance
- monitoring schedule variance
- identifying project risks
- recommending corrective actions
- summarising project progress

AI assists people rather than replacing them.

---

# AI Capabilities

## Project Health Analysis

AI continuously evaluates:

- schedule performance
- cost performance
- milestone completion
- deliverable status
- project risks
- issue trends
- dependency impacts

Outputs include an overall Project Health Score with supporting explanations.

---

## Schedule Forecasting

Artificial Intelligence analyses:

- historical delivery performance
- resource availability
- milestone completion
- dependency chains
- project complexity

Outputs include:

- predicted completion date
- confidence level
- delay probability

---

## Budget Forecasting

AI evaluates:

- approved budget
- actual expenditure
- forecast expenditure
- burn rate
- committed costs

Outputs include:

- projected final cost
- variance forecast
- overspend risk

---

## Risk Prediction

AI identifies:

- emerging delivery risks
- schedule risks
- resource conflicts
- dependency failures
- governance risks
- financial risks

Risks are prioritised based on probability and potential business impact.

---

## Resource Optimisation

AI analyses:

- resource allocation
- utilisation
- capacity
- skills availability
- workload distribution

Recommendations may include:

- reallocation
- additional staffing
- schedule adjustments

---

## Executive Summaries

AI automatically generates concise summaries including:

- overall project health
- completed milestones
- upcoming milestones
- critical risks
- financial position
- recommended executive actions

Summaries are intended for governance meetings and portfolio reviews.

---

## Lessons Learned Analysis

AI reviews completed projects to identify:

- recurring risks
- delivery patterns
- successful practices
- governance improvements
- planning improvements

These insights contribute to continuous organisational learning.

---

# AI Inputs

Artificial Intelligence consumes governed information including:

- Project Status
- Budget
- Milestones
- Deliverables
- Risks
- Issues
- Resource Assignments
- Dependencies
- Change Requests
- Audit History

AI only consumes validated Project data.

---

# AI Outputs

AI may generate:

- health scores
- forecasts
- recommendations
- summaries
- alerts
- confidence scores
- optimisation suggestions

Outputs remain linked to their supporting evidence.

---

# Human Oversight

The following decisions remain exclusively human:

- project approval
- budget approval
- scope approval
- contract approval
- stage gate approval
- project cancellation
- project closure

AI may support these decisions but never execute them.

---

# Explainability

Every AI recommendation should include:

- recommendation
- supporting evidence
- confidence score
- contributing factors
- recommended actions

Users should understand why AI reached a conclusion.

---

# Governance

AI usage requires:

- approved models
- governed datasets
- audit logging
- security enforcement
- human accountability

AI activity must be fully traceable.

---

# AI Summary

The AI Usage Model transforms the Project Business Object into an intelligent execution capability within Swissbay Nexus.

By combining governed project information with predictive analytics, explainable recommendations and executive insights, Artificial Intelligence improves delivery confidence while preserving human governance, accountability and organisational control.
