# 09 — Business Rules

---

# Overview

The Business Rules define the governance policies controlling every Project throughout its lifecycle.

These rules ensure that Projects are created, planned, executed and closed consistently while maintaining accountability, financial control and organisational governance.

Business Rules define what is permitted.

Validation confirms whether those rules have been satisfied.

---

# Purpose

The Project Business Rules exist to:

- standardise project governance
- strengthen accountability
- improve delivery consistency
- reduce project risk
- support executive oversight
- enable Artificial Intelligence
- maintain complete auditability

Every Project interaction must comply with these rules.

---

# Rule Categories

Project Business Rules are organised into:

- Identity Rules
- Governance Rules
- Financial Rules
- Resource Rules
- Lifecycle Rules
- Delivery Rules
- Security Rules
- AI Rules
- Audit Rules

---

# Identity Rules

## BR-PROJ-001

Every Project must have one unique Project Identifier.

---

## BR-PROJ-002

Project Identifiers:

- cannot be modified
- cannot be reused
- remain permanent

---

## BR-PROJ-003

Every Project must contain:

- Project Identifier
- Project Name
- Project Sponsor
- Business Owner
- Project Manager
- Status

---

## BR-PROJ-004

Duplicate Projects are prohibited.

Duplicate detection should consider:

- Project Name
- Customer
- Contract
- Business Owner

---

# Governance Rules

## BR-PROJ-005

Every Project must have an approved Sponsor.

---

## BR-PROJ-006

Projects must progress through the approved Project Lifecycle.

---

## BR-PROJ-007

Stage Gate approval is required before progressing to the next governed phase.

---

## BR-PROJ-008

Projects without an assigned Project Manager may not enter Execution.

---

# Financial Rules

## BR-PROJ-009

Projects requiring financial investment must have an approved budget.

---

## BR-PROJ-010

Budget changes require an approved Change Request.

---

## BR-PROJ-011

Actual costs must be traceable to approved financial records.

---

# Resource Rules

## BR-PROJ-012

Every assigned resource must reference a governed Employee or approved external resource.

---

## BR-PROJ-013

Resource assignments must not exceed approved capacity without governance approval.

---

# Delivery Rules

## BR-PROJ-014

Every Deliverable must have:

- owner
- target completion date
- acceptance criteria

---

## BR-PROJ-015

Completed Deliverables require formal acceptance where governance applies.

---

## BR-PROJ-016

All Risks, Issues and Change Requests must remain linked to the Project.

---

# Lifecycle Rules

## BR-PROJ-017

Projects may exist in only one lifecycle stage at a time.

---

## BR-PROJ-018

Archived Projects become read-only.

---

# Security Rules

## BR-PROJ-019

Access follows role-based security.

---

## BR-PROJ-020

Project financial information may only be viewed by authorised users.

---

# AI Rules

## BR-PROJ-021

Artificial Intelligence may:

- forecast schedules
- analyse risks
- recommend resources
- estimate delivery confidence
- generate executive summaries

---

## BR-PROJ-022

Artificial Intelligence may not:

- approve budgets
- close Projects
- approve Stage Gates
- override governance
- bypass Business Rules

Human accountability remains mandatory.

---

# Audit Rules

## BR-PROJ-023

Every significant Project change must record:

- user
- timestamp
- previous value
- new value
- business reason

---

## BR-PROJ-024

Budget changes, scope changes, governance decisions and lifecycle transitions must always be auditable.

---

# Rule Priorities

| Priority | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Critical | Operation blocked |
| High | Immediate correction required |
| Medium | Warning issued |
| Low | Advisory only |

# Rule Enforcement

Business Rules are enforced through:

- Validation Engine
- Workflow Engine
- Security Engine
- Automation Engine
- API Gateway
- AI Governance Engine

Rules remain independent of implementation technology.

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions require:

- documented business justification
- executive approval
- governance review
- expiry date
- audit record

Temporary exceptions must never become permanent operating behaviour.

---

# Business Rules Summary

The Project Business Rules establish the governance framework for enterprise project execution within Swissbay Nexus.

By defining consistent rules for governance, budgeting, resources, lifecycle progression, delivery, Artificial Intelligence and auditability, Swissbay ensures that Projects remain trusted, controlled and strategically aligned throughout their lifecycle.
