# OBJ-006 — Project

## Overview

The Project Business Object defines the governed representation of every project managed within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Projects represent the execution layer of the enterprise, transforming strategic objectives, contractual commitments and operational initiatives into measurable business outcomes.

The Project Business Object provides a single source of truth for project identity, governance, planning, execution, monitoring, delivery and closure while maintaining complete visibility across resources, budgets, timelines, risks, issues and deliverables.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Project Business Object is to establish one canonical representation of every project across Swissbay Nexus.

Projects may include:

- Customer Implementation Projects
- Internal Improvement Projects
- Capital Projects
- Construction Projects
- Mining Projects
- Technology Projects
- Infrastructure Projects
- Research & Development
- Business Transformation
- Compliance Initiatives

Every project follows the same governed lifecycle regardless of methodology or business unit.
