# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Sales Order Roadmap defines the long-term strategic evolution of the Sales Order Business Object within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

It provides a structured vision for enhancing commercial transaction management while maintaining governance, security, interoperability and backwards compatibility.

The roadmap guides future development but does not replace governance, project planning or release management.

---

# Purpose

The Sales Order Roadmap exists to:

- provide strategic direction
- improve commercial operations
- strengthen Quote-to-Cash capabilities
- support enterprise growth
- enable Artificial Intelligence
- improve customer experience
- expand enterprise integrations

The roadmap ensures Sales Order capabilities evolve in a controlled and governed manner.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Future Sales Order enhancements should always be:

- business-driven
- reusable
- secure
- governed
- measurable
- AI-ready
- backwards compatible where practical

Every enhancement should increase business capability while preserving platform consistency.

# Roadmap Summary

The Sales Order Roadmap provides the long-term strategic vision for commercial transaction management within Swissbay Nexus.
