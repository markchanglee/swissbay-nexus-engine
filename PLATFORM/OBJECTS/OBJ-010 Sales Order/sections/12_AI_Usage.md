# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is a governed participant within the Sales Order Business Object.

Rather than replacing Sales Operations, Finance, Warehouse personnel or Customer Service representatives, AI enhances commercial decision-making by analysing transactional data, identifying patterns, forecasting outcomes and recommending actions.

Every AI capability operates within the governance boundaries defined by the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000), Security Standard (SEC-000) and Governance Framework.

Artificial Intelligence supports business users.

It never owns Sales Orders nor makes autonomous commercial decisions.

---

# Purpose

The AI Usage Model exists to:

- improve order processing
- improve fulfilment
- improve customer experience
- forecast revenue
- optimise warehouse operations
- reduce commercial risk
- support executives
- improve operational efficiency

AI exists to augment business users while preserving governance and accountability.

---

# AI Responsibilities

Artificial Intelligence may support the Sales Order lifecycle by:

- analysing order patterns

- recommendation
- confidence score
- supporting Sales Orders
- supporting Business Rules
- supporting KPIs

- alternative recommendations

---

# Confidence Levels

Swissbay classifies AI confidence as:

| Confidence | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| 95–100% | Very High |
| 85–94% | High |
| 70–84% | Moderate |
| Below 70% | Low |

Low-confidence recommendations require additional human review.

---

# AI Restrictions

Artificial Intelligence may not:

- approve Sales Orders
- modify pricing
- create invoices
- authorise discounts
- cancel orders
- override Business Rules
- bypass commercial approvals
- expose confidential customer information

Commercial accountability always remains with authorised business users.

---

# AI Security

Artificial Intelligence must:

- inherit user permissions
- respect Sales Order classifications
- protect customer information
- maintain audit history
- explain recommendations

AI security follows SEC-000.

---

# AI Governance

- Business Owner

# AI Usage Summary

The Sales Order Business Object enables Artificial Intelligence to become an intelligent commercial advisor within Swissbay Nexus.

By combining trusted transactional information, governed Business Rules, explainable recommendations and continuous monitoring, Swissbay Nexus delivers AI-assisted order management that improves customer experience, fulfilment efficiency, revenue forecasting and operational decision-making while preserving governance, security and human accountability.
