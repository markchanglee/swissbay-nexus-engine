# 03 — Business Value

---

# Overview

The Sales Order Business Object provides the governed foundation for managing every commercial sales transaction within Swissbay Nexus.

Every Sales Order represents a legally and commercially significant business event that connects customers, products, pricing, inventory, fulfilment and finance into a single governed transaction.

By establishing one enterprise-wide definition of a Sales Order, Swissbay enables consistent commercial operations across every department and technology platform.

The Sales Order Business Object transforms customer purchases into trusted enterprise transactions.
