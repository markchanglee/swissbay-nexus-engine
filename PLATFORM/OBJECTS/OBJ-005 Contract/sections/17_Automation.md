# 17 — Automation

---

# Overview

The Contract Automation Model defines how contract lifecycle activities are automated within Swissbay Nexus.

Automation reduces manual effort while preserving Legal and business accountability.

# Automation Details

AUTO-CON-001 routes contract requests to the correct legal or commercial owner.

AUTO-CON-002 suggests approved templates based on contract type and counterparty.

AUTO-CON-003 assigns Legal, Finance, Procurement, Sales or HR reviewers.

AUTO-CON-004 determines approval path based on value, risk, counterparty, deviations and contract type.

AUTO-CON-005 notifies authorised signatories when execution is ready.

AUTO-CON-006 alerts obligation owners before due dates.

AUTO-CON-007 notifies owners before renewal notice dates and expiry.

AUTO-CON-008 detects expired or soon-to-expire contracts.

AUTO-CON-009 generates advisory summaries, risks and obligation lists.

AUTO-CON-010 archives completed, expired or terminated Contracts according to retention policy.

---

# Security

Automations must respect role permissions, confidentiality and audit requirements.

---

# Automation Summary

Contract automation enables proactive governance for reviews, approvals, obligations, renewals and compliance.
