# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is a governed participant within the Contract Business Object.

AI supports contract intelligence by analysing documents, extracting obligations, summarising agreements, identifying risk and recommending actions.

AI never replaces Legal, Commercial or Executive approval.

---

# AI Capabilities

| Capability | Supported |
|---|---|
| Contract Summarisation | ✅ |
| Clause Extraction | ✅ |
| Obligation Extraction | ✅ |
| Risk Analysis | ✅ |
| Renewal Forecasting | ✅ |
| Compliance Monitoring | ✅ |
| Contract Comparison | ✅ |
| Contract Approval | ❌ |
| Contract Execution | ❌ |
| Legal Advice Finalisation | ❌ |
| Contract Termination | ❌ |

---

# AI During Lifecycle

AI may support request classification, draft review, clause comparison, risk detection, obligation extraction, renewal recommendations and archival summarisation.

---

# AI Inputs

AI consumes governed Contract records, documents, obligations, counterparties, approvals, amendments, KPIs, dashboards and Business Rules.

---

# AI Outputs

AI may generate summaries, risk reports, obligation registers, renewal alerts, clause deviation reports and executive briefs.

---

# Explainability

AI recommendations must include confidence score, supporting clauses, related Business Rules, assumptions and alternative recommendations.

---

# AI Restrictions

AI may not approve, sign, amend, terminate, negotiate independently, provide final legal advice or bypass governance.

---

# AI Security

AI must inherit user permissions, protect confidential information, respect legal privilege and maintain audit history.

---

# AI Usage Summary

The Contract Business Object enables AI-assisted contract management while preserving legal accountability and human governance.
