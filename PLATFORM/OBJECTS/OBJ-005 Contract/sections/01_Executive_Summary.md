# 01 — Executive Summary

---

# Overview

The Contract Business Object is the canonical representation of every legally binding agreement managed within Swissbay Nexus.

A Contract establishes enforceable commitments between two or more parties and governs the commercial, legal, operational and financial relationships that enable enterprise execution.

Unlike traditional Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) systems that primarily manage documents, Swissbay models Contracts as intelligent enterprise business objects capable of driving workflows, automation, Artificial Intelligence, compliance monitoring and operational delivery.

The signed contract document is only one component of the governed Contract Business Object.

---

# Enterprise Vision

Swissbay treats every Contract as an enterprise asset rather than a stored document.

Every Contract should be:

- Governed

- Knowledge Graph Connected

This enables Contracts to actively participate in enterprise operations throughout their lifecycle.
