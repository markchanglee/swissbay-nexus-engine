# Dashboard Purposes

The Contract Overview Dashboard answers how many contracts exist, which are active, which are expiring, which require review and who owns them.

The Contract Risk Dashboard highlights high-risk contracts, non-standard clauses, expired agreements, overdue obligations and unusual terms.

The Renewal Dashboard monitors contracts approaching expiry, renewal windows, auto-renewals and owner actions.

The Obligation Dashboard tracks obligation owners, due dates, completion status and evidence.

The Compliance Dashboard monitors contractual compliance, policy exceptions and audit readiness.

The Executive Contract Dashboard provides strategic visibility into major contracts, risk exposure, value, expiry and obligations.

The AI Contract Insights Dashboard displays AI summaries, risk alerts, clause deviations, obligation extraction and recommended actions.

---

# Filters

Dashboards should support filtering by contract type, owner, counterparty, status, expiry date, risk rating, department and value.

---

# Security

Dashboard access follows Contract confidentiality and role-based access rules.

---

# Dashboard Summary

Contract dashboards provide actionable intelligence for legal governance, commercial control, risk management and executive oversight.
