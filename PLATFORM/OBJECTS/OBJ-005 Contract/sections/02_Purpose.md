# 02 — Purpose

---

# Overview

The Contract Business Object exists to provide a single, governed representation of every legally binding agreement throughout its complete enterprise lifecycle.

It enables organisations to manage contractual relationships consistently while improving governance, compliance, operational execution, commercial performance and enterprise intelligence.

The Contract Business Object standardises agreement management across business units, departments, technologies and industries.

---

# Why the Contract Business Object Exists

Most organisations manage contracts across disconnected systems including:

- document repositories
- shared drives
- email
- spreadsheets
- ERP systems
- CRM platforms
- legal applications

This fragmentation creates significant operational and commercial risk.

Common challenges include:

- duplicate contracts
- inconsistent approval processes
- missed renewal dates
- unmanaged obligations
- poor version control
- limited visibility
- weak compliance
- revenue leakage
- audit failures

The Contract Business Object addresses these challenges by providing one canonical enterprise model for agreement governance.
