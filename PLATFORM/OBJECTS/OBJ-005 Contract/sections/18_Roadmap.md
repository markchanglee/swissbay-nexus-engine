# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Contract Roadmap defines the strategic evolution of the Contract Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

---

# Current State

Version 1.0.0 provides a governed Contract Business Object with lifecycle, data model, relationships, rules, workflows, dashboards, KPIs, validation, security, automation and AI usage.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

Completed capabilities include Contract Business Object, Contract Lifecycle, Contract Data Model, Contract Workflows, Security, Automation and AI Integration.

---

# Phase 2 — Contract Governance Excellence

Objectives include obligation register, renewal calendar, clause library, template governance, contract risk scoring and compliance monitoring.

---

# Phase 3 — Intelligent Contracting

Objectives include AI clause extraction, AI risk detection, contract summarisation, obligation intelligence, renewal forecasting and contract comparison.

---

# Phase 4 — Connected Enterprise

Integrate with CRM, ERP, HRIS, procurement, finance, e-signature, document management and project systems.

---

# Phase 5 — Digital Contract Operations

Introduce self-service contract requests, digital signing, obligation dashboards, contract portals and automated evidence collection.

---

# Phase 6 — Autonomous Contract Intelligence

Future capabilities may include contract digital twins, AI negotiation support, predictive contract risk and autonomous renewal recommendations. Human approval remains mandatory.

---

# AI Roadmap

Future AI capabilities include conversational contract assistant, clause recommendation, contract risk forecasting, obligation monitoring and legal knowledge graph.

---

# KPI Roadmap

Future KPIs may include obligation risk index, clause deviation rate, negotiation efficiency, legal review productivity and AI risk accuracy.

---

# Governance

Roadmap updates require Legal Review, Architecture Review and Governance Approval.

---

# Roadmap Summary

The Contract Roadmap guides the evolution of Swissbay contract governance from static agreement management to intelligent, governed contract operations.
