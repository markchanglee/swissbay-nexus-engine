# Overview

The Version History records the controlled evolution of the Contract Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

Every published version represents an approved enterprise standard.

# Purpose

Version History exists to document Contract Business Object evolution, preserve governance decisions, support audit requirements, maintain backwards compatibility and improve architecture transparency.

# Breaking Change Policy

Breaking changes require impact assessment, architecture review, integration review, governance approval, communication plan and migration strategy.

---

# Governance Approval

Every new version requires Legal, Enterprise Architecture, Platform Governance and Information Security approval where applicable.

---

# Review Cycle

The Contract Business Object should be reviewed annually or after major legal, commercial, regulatory or platform changes.
