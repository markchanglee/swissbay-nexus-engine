# 10 — Workflows

---

# Overview

The Contract Workflow Model defines how contracts are requested, drafted, reviewed, approved, executed, amended, renewed, terminated and archived.

# Workflow Details

WF-CON-001 captures the need for a new Contract and assigns ownership.

WF-CON-002 creates the draft using approved templates or negotiated input.

WF-CON-003 allows Legal to review risk, clauses, obligations, liability, jurisdiction and compliance.

WF-CON-004 allows Sales, Procurement or Finance to review commercial terms, pricing, value and payment terms.

WF-CON-005 routes required approvers before execution.

WF-CON-006 records authorised signature or acceptance.

WF-CON-007 tracks obligations, deadlines, evidence and responsible owners.

WF-CON-008 manages governed changes to executed Contracts.

WF-CON-009 reviews Contracts approaching expiry for renewal, renegotiation or termination.

WF-CON-010 manages controlled Contract termination.

---

# AI Participation

AI may summarise contracts, identify risky clauses, extract obligations and recommend review actions. AI may not approve, execute or terminate contracts.

---

# Workflow Summary

Contract workflows provide the operational foundation for legal and commercial governance.
