# 14 — Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

---

# Overview

Contract KPIs measure contract governance, legal performance, commercial control, renewal effectiveness and compliance.

---

# KPI Catalogue

| KPI ID | KPI Name | Category |
|---|---|---|
| KPI-CON-001 | Active Contracts | Operational |
| KPI-CON-002 | Contract Cycle Time | Operational |
| KPI-CON-003 | Renewal Compliance Rate | Governance |
| KPI-CON-004 | Obligation Completion Rate | Compliance |
| KPI-CON-005 | Contract Risk Score | Risk |
| KPI-CON-006 | Expired Contract Count | Risk |
| KPI-CON-007 | Amendment Rate | Governance |
| KPI-CON-008 | Approval Cycle Time | Operational |
| KPI-CON-009 | AI Risk Detection Accuracy | AI |
| KPI-CON-010 | Contract Value Under Management | Financial |

---

# KPI Definitions

KPI-CON-001 measures Contracts with status Active.

KPI-CON-002 measures time from Contract Request to Execution.

KPI-CON-003 measures percentage of Contracts reviewed before renewal or expiry date.

KPI-CON-004 measures completed obligations divided by total due obligations.

KPI-CON-005 is a composite risk score based on value, clauses, counterparty, obligations and deviations.

KPI-CON-006 counts Contracts expired without renewal, termination or archival action.

KPI-CON-007 measures frequency of amendments across Contracts.

KPI-CON-008 measures time from review completion to final approval.

KPI-CON-009 measures AI risk predictions against legal review outcomes.

KPI-CON-010 measures total value of active Contracts.

---

# KPI Ownership

Legal owns most contract KPIs. Finance owns value-related KPIs. AI Governance owns AI accuracy KPIs.

---

# KPI Summary

Contract KPIs provide measurable insight into legal governance, compliance, commercial risk and contract performance.
