# Department Roles

Legal owns contract governance, review, risk, approval standards and legal compliance.

Procurement uses Contracts to govern suppliers, pricing, service levels, delivery and obligations.

Sales uses Contracts to govern customer terms, commercial commitments, discounts and service obligations.

Finance reviews financial terms, payment terms, revenue impact, contract value and audit requirements.

Human Resources uses Contracts for employment agreements, contractor terms and workforce-related obligations.

Operations uses Contracts to understand service commitments, delivery obligations and performance requirements.

Executives review strategic agreements, risk exposure, major obligations and commercial commitments.

AI supports contract analysis, obligation extraction, renewal alerts and risk summaries while remaining advisory.

# Department Usage Summary

The Contract Business Object enables departments to collaborate from one governed source of contract truth.
