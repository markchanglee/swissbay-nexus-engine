# OBJ-003 — Product

## Overview

The Product Business Object defines the governed representation of every product, service, material or offering managed within Swissbay Nexus.

Products form the commercial bridge between Suppliers, Warehouses, Projects, Sales Orders, Assets and Customers.

The Product Business Object provides a single source of truth for product identity, classification, pricing, inventory attributes, lifecycle and relationships.

It enables Procurement, Sales, Operations, Finance, Manufacturing, Artificial Intelligence and Executive Management to work from one governed product definition.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Product Business Object is to establish a canonical definition of every product managed by Swissbay.

Rather than allowing multiple systems to maintain inconsistent product information, Swissbay governs products through a single reusable Business Object.
