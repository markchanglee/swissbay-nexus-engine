# 14 — Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

---

# Overview

The Product Business Object provides the enterprise performance measures used to evaluate products throughout their lifecycle.

These KPIs support operational management, financial reporting, strategic planning and Artificial Intelligence by providing consistent, governed measurements of product performance.

Every KPI defined within this document follows the Swissbay KPI Standard (KPI-000).

---

# Purpose

The Product KPI Model exists to:

- measure product performance
- improve profitability
- optimise inventory
- support executive reporting
- improve operational planning
- enable AI insights
- drive continuous improvement

KPIs measure business outcomes rather than business activity.

# KPI-PROD-001 — Active Product Count

## Purpose

Measures the number of Products currently available for operational use.

### Formula

```
Count(Products)

WHERE

Lifecycle = Active
```

### Frequency

Daily

### Target

Business Defined

---

# KPI-PROD-002 — Product Revenue

## Purpose

Measures revenue generated by each Product.

### Formula

```
Sum(Product Revenue)
```

### Frequency

Daily

### Used By

- Finance
- Executive
- Sales

---

# KPI-PROD-003 — Product Profit Margin

## Purpose

Measures commercial profitability.

### Formula

```
(Product Revenue

-

Product Cost)

÷

Product Revenue

×

100
```

### Target

Business Defined

---

# KPI-PROD-004 — Inventory Turnover

## Purpose

Measures how efficiently inventory is utilised.

### Formula

```
Cost of Goods Sold

÷

Average Inventory Value
```

### Target

Business Defined

Inventory ownership remains with the Warehouse Business Object.

---

# KPI-PROD-005 — Product Availability

## Purpose

Measures the percentage of time a Product is available for sale or operational use.

### Formula

```
Available Products

÷

Total Active Products

×

100
```

### Target

≥ 98%

---

# KPI-PROD-006 — Product Lifecycle Duration

## Purpose

Measures the average time Products remain in each lifecycle stage.

### Inputs

- Draft Date
- Approval Date
- Active Date
- Retirement Date

Used to identify lifecycle bottlenecks.

---

# KPI-PROD-007 — Product Approval Cycle Time

## Purpose

Measures the efficiency of Product governance.

### Formula

```
Approval Date

-

Draft Date
```

### Target

Business Defined

---

# KPI-PROD-008 — Product Demand Forecast Accuracy

## Purpose

Measures the accuracy of AI demand forecasting.

### Formula

```
Forecast Accuracy

=

Forecast Demand

vs

Actual Demand
```

### Frequency

Monthly

### Audience

- Operations
- AI Governance
- Executive

---

# KPI-PROD-009 — Product Return Rate

## Purpose

Measures Product quality through customer or operational returns.

### Formula

```
Returned Products

÷

Products Sold

×

100
```

### Target

Business Defined

Lower values indicate better product quality.

---

# KPI-PROD-010 — Product Portfolio Growth

## Purpose

Measures the growth of the governed Product catalogue.

### Formula

```
New Active Products

-

Retired Products
```

### Frequency

Monthly

Supports strategic portfolio planning.

Product KPIs are displayed within:

# AI Integration

Artificial Intelligence may:

- analyse KPI trends
- explain KPI movements
- forecast future KPI values
- identify anomalies
- recommend corrective actions
- benchmark Products

AI recommendations reference governed KPI definitions.

---

# KPI Validation

Before approval verify that:

- KPI purpose defined
- calculation documented
- ownership assigned
- reporting frequency defined
- thresholds documented
- data source governed

---

# KPI Governance

Changes to KPI definitions require:

- Product Management Review
- Finance Review (where applicable)
- Governance Approval
- Version Update

KPI definitions remain stable and reusable across the platform.

---

# KPI Summary

The Product KPI Portfolio provides the official performance measurements for product management within Swissbay Nexus.

By defining consistent, governed and measurable indicators, Swissbay enables Product Management, Operations, Finance, Warehousing, Executive Management and Artificial Intelligence to evaluate product performance objectively, optimise operations and support strategic decision-making.

Product KPIs transform operational activity into measurable business outcomes that drive enterprise performance.
