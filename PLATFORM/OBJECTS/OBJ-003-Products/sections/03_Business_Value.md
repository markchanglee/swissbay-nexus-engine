# 03 — Business Value

---

# Overview

The Product Business Object provides the governed foundation for managing every product, service, material and commercial offering within Swissbay Nexus.

Products represent the value that the organisation creates, acquires, manages and ultimately delivers to customers.

By establishing a single, enterprise-wide definition of every product, Swissbay enables consistent operations across procurement, inventory, warehousing, finance, sales, projects and customer fulfilment.

The Product Business Object transforms products from isolated application records into strategic enterprise assets.

---

Products become reusable enterprise knowledge rather than isolated system records.

# Value Summary

The Product Business Object is the commercial heart of Swissbay Nexus.
