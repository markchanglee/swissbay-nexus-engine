# 01 — Executive Summary

---

# Overview

The Product Business Object defines the standard for managing every product, service, material and commercial offering within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Products represent the core value exchanged between Swissbay and its customers. They connect suppliers, inventory, projects, warehouses, sales, finance and customers into a single governed business ecosystem.

The Product Business Object provides one authoritative definition of every product, ensuring that all departments, applications, dashboards, workflows and Artificial Intelligence operate from the same trusted information.

It serves as the canonical source of product information across the enterprise.

---

# Business Purpose

The purpose of the Product Business Object is to establish a governed, reusable and enterprise-wide definition of every product managed by Swissbay.

Rather than allowing multiple applications to maintain separate product catalogues, Swissbay manages products through a single Business Object that is referenced throughout the platform.

This ensures:

- one version of the truth
- consistent product definitions
- improved inventory accuracy
- standardised pricing
- simplified reporting
- improved customer service
- trusted AI analysis

---

# Business Importance

Products sit at the centre of nearly every business process.

Every department depends on trusted product information.

Examples include:

- Procurement purchases products from suppliers.
- Warehouses store products.
- Projects consume products.
- Sales sell products.
- Finance values products.
- Customers purchase products.
- AI analyses product demand and profitability.

Without governed product information, operational efficiency, reporting accuracy and customer satisfaction decline significantly.

# Strategic Value

The Product Business Object supports Swissbay's long-term strategy by:

- creating a governed product catalogue

Products become strategic business assets rather than isolated database records.
