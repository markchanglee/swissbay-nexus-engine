# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence is a governed participant within the Product Business Object.

Rather than replacing product managers, procurement specialists or sales teams, AI augments decision-making by analysing product information, identifying trends, generating recommendations and supporting operational planning.

Every AI capability operates within the boundaries defined by the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000), Security Standard (SEC-000) and Governance Framework.

AI is an intelligent business assistant—not the owner of Product information.

---

# Purpose

The AI Usage Model exists to:

- improve product quality
- optimise inventory
- forecast demand
- improve profitability
- reduce duplication
- support procurement
- improve customer experience
- assist business users

AI always supports human judgement.

---

# Explainability

Every significant AI recommendation should include:

- recommendation
- confidence score
- supporting Business Objects
- supporting KPIs
- supporting Business Rules
- assumptions
- alternative options

Recommendations should always be explainable.

---

# Confidence Levels

Swissbay classifies AI confidence as follows.

| Confidence | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| 95–100% | Very High |
| 85–94% | High |
| 70–84% | Moderate |
| Below 70% | Low |

Low-confidence recommendations should require additional review.

---

# AI Restrictions

Artificial Intelligence may not:

- approve Products
- modify Product ownership
- alter Product lifecycle
- bypass Business Rules
- expose confidential commercial information
- delete Products
- create unauthorised relationships

Human accountability remains mandatory.

---

# AI Security

AI must:

- inherit user permissions
- respect product classifications
- protect commercial information
- explain recommendations
- maintain audit history

AI security follows SEC-000.

---

# AI Governance

Every AI capability requires:

- Business Owner
- Technical Owner
- Data Sources
- Review Frequency
- Monitoring
- Governance Approval

AI capabilities remain governed enterprise assets.

# AI Usage Summary

The Product Business Object enables Artificial Intelligence to become an intelligent product management advisor while maintaining governance, security and human accountability.
