# 02 — Purpose

---

# Purpose Statement

The Product Business Object exists to establish a single, governed representation of every product, service, material and commercial offering managed within Swissbay Nexus.

It provides the authoritative source of product information across Procurement, Operations, Warehouse, Sales, Finance, Projects and Artificial Intelligence.

The Product Business Object eliminates fragmented product catalogues and creates one trusted enterprise definition that supports every downstream business process.

---

# Business Problem

Without a governed Product Business Object organisations typically experience:

- duplicate product records
- inconsistent product descriptions
- inaccurate inventory
- conflicting pricing
- poor reporting
- disconnected procurement
- unreliable forecasting
- inconsistent customer experiences

These issues increase operational costs and reduce business agility.

Swissbay addresses these challenges through one governed Product Business Object.

---

# Business Objective

The Product Business Object enables Swissbay to:

- maintain one trusted product catalogue
- standardise product definitions
- improve inventory accuracy
- support procurement
- improve warehouse operations
- enable sales
- improve financial reporting
- provide trusted AI data
- enable enterprise reporting

---

# Strategic Purpose

The Product Business Object contributes to Swissbay's long-term strategy by:

- supporting enterprise growth
- improving operational efficiency
- enabling digital commerce
- supporting multiple business units
- enabling international expansion
- improving customer experience
- providing trusted product intelligence

Products become governed business assets rather than application records.

# Operational Purpose

The Product Business Object supports daily operations by providing one consistent framework for:

- product creation
- product approval
- product classification
- pricing
- inventory management
- supplier association
- warehouse management
- sales fulfilment
- product retirement

Every operational process references the same Product Business Object.

---

# Relationship to the Swissbay Operating Model

The Product Business Object is the commercial centre of Swissbay Nexus.

It directly connects:

- Suppliers
- Warehouses
- Customers
- Sales Orders
- Projects
- Assets
- Contracts
- Opportunities

Rather than duplicating product information across these Business Objects, each references the governed Product Business Object.

# Purpose Summary

The Product Business Object exists to provide a governed, reusable and trusted representation of every product within Swissbay Nexus.
