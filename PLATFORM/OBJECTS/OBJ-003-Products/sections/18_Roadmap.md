# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Product Roadmap defines the strategic evolution of the Product Business Object within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

It provides a long-term vision for expanding Product capabilities while maintaining governance, security, interoperability and backwards compatibility.

The roadmap guides future development but does not replace governance or release management.

---

# Purpose

The Product Roadmap exists to:

- provide strategic direction
- support continuous improvement
- enable innovation
- improve operational maturity
- prepare for Artificial Intelligence
- strengthen enterprise integration
- support industry expansion

The roadmap ensures that Product management evolves in a controlled and governed manner.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Future Product enhancements should always be:

- business-driven
- reusable
- secure
- governed
- backwards compatible where practical
- measurable
- AI-ready

Every enhancement should improve enterprise capability rather than increase unnecessary complexity.

# Success Criteria

The Product Roadmap will be considered successful when:

- Product information remains trusted
- commercial visibility improves

# Governance

- Product Management Review
- Architecture Review
- Governance Approval
- Version Update

The roadmap should be reviewed annually or following major platform releases.

# Roadmap Summary

The Product Roadmap provides the long-term strategic vision for Product management within Swissbay Nexus.

By progressively introducing advanced analytics, intelligent automation, AI-assisted decision-making, enterprise integrations and digital commerce capabilities, Swissbay ensures that the Product Business Object continues to deliver increasing business value while remaining governed, secure and aligned with organisational strategy.
