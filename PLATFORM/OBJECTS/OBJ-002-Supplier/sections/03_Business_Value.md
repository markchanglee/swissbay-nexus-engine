# 03 — Business Value

---

# Overview

The Supplier Business Object creates value by providing a governed, centralised and reusable representation of every supplier within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Rather than maintaining supplier information across multiple departments, systems or spreadsheets, Swissbay manages suppliers through one authoritative Business Object that supports procurement, finance, operations, legal, executive management and Artificial Intelligence.

This object transforms supplier information into a strategic business asset.

---

# Strategic Business Value

The Supplier Business Object strengthens Swissbay's long-term business strategy by:

- creating resilient supplier ecosystems
- enabling strategic sourcing
- improving supplier collaboration
- supporting business growth
- reducing supply chain risk
- enabling enterprise-wide visibility
- supporting multi-company operations
- creating trusted procurement intelligence

By governing supplier information centrally, Swissbay improves both operational performance and executive decision-making.
