# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Supplier Roadmap defines the planned evolution of the Supplier Business Object throughout the Swissbay Nexus platform.

It provides a strategic direction for future capabilities while ensuring that supplier management continues to support changing business requirements, emerging technologies and evolving procurement practices.

The roadmap is a planning document.

It guides future development but does not replace governance.

---

# Purpose

The Supplier Roadmap exists to:

- provide long-term direction
- support continuous improvement
- enable innovation
- improve procurement maturity
- prepare for AI advancements
- strengthen supplier relationships
- support enterprise scalability

The roadmap ensures that supplier management evolves in a controlled and governed manner.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Supplier enhancements should always be:

- business driven
- backwards compatible where practical
- governed
- measurable
- reusable
- secure
- AI-ready

Every enhancement should improve business capability rather than increase complexity.

# Success Criteria

The Supplier Roadmap will be considered successful when:

- supplier information remains trusted
- procurement becomes increasingly automated
- supplier risk is proactively managed
- AI recommendations become more accurate
- supplier collaboration improves
- procurement supports enterprise growth

Success is measured through business outcomes rather than technology adoption.

---

# Governance

Roadmap updates require:

- Procurement Review
- Architecture Review
- Governance Approval
- Version Update

The roadmap should be reviewed annually or following significant platform releases.

---

# Roadmap Summary

The Supplier Roadmap provides the long-term strategic vision for the evolution of supplier management within Swissbay Nexus.

By progressively introducing intelligent procurement capabilities, enhanced collaboration, enterprise integrations and AI-driven insights, Swissbay ensures that the Supplier Business Object continues to deliver increasing business value while remaining governed, secure and aligned with the organisation's strategic objectives.
