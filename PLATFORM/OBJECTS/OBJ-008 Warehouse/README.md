# OBJ-008 — Warehouse

## Overview

The Warehouse Business Object defines the governed representation of every physical, virtual and logical storage location managed within the Swissbay Nexus platform.

Warehouses are responsible for receiving, storing, protecting, issuing and transferring enterprise resources including inventory, spare parts, consumables, equipment and operational assets.

The Warehouse Business Object provides the single source of truth for warehouse identity, storage locations, operational status, inventory capacity, movement history and governance.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of the Warehouse Business Object is to establish one canonical representation of enterprise warehouses across Swissbay Nexus.

Warehouses may include:

- Distribution Centres
- Mining Stores
- Manufacturing Warehouses
- Spare Parts Stores
- Vehicle Depots
- IT Equipment Stores
- Pharmaceutical Stores
- Cold Storage Facilities
- Bonded Warehouses
- Virtual Warehouses
- Transit Warehouses
- Project Stores

Every Warehouse follows the same governed operational model regardless of industry or location.
