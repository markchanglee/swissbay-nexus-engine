# 19 — Version History

---

# Overview

The Version History records the controlled evolution of the Warehouse Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

It provides a complete audit trail of structural, semantic and governance changes made throughout the lifecycle of the Warehouse Business Object.

Every published version represents an approved enterprise standard.

---

# Purpose

The Version History exists to:

- document Business Object evolution
- preserve governance decisions
- support audit requirements
- communicate platform improvements
- maintain backwards compatibility
- improve architectural transparency

Version History forms part of the permanent governance record.

---

# Versioning Standard

The Warehouse Business Object follows Semantic Versioning.

```text
MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH
```

Example

```text
2.1.3
```

| Version Element | Meaning |
|-----------------|---------|
| Major | Breaking business or structural changes |
| Minor | New capabilities with backwards compatibility |
| Patch | Corrections and documentation updates |

# Breaking Change Policy

Breaking changes require:

- Business Impact Assessment
- Architecture Review
- Integration Assessment
- Governance Approval
- Migration Strategy
- Communication Plan

Breaking changes should be minimised.

---

# Backwards Compatibility

Minor and patch releases should remain backwards compatible.

Compatibility applies to:

- APIs
- workflows
- Business Rules
- dashboards
- KPIs
- integrations
- automation

Migration guidance must accompany breaking changes.

---

- AI Governance Board (where applicable)

# Retirement Policy

If the Warehouse Business Object is superseded:

- a successor Business Object must be identified
- migration guidance must be published
- historical Warehouse records remain accessible
- audit obligations continue
- archived versions remain available

Enterprise warehouse history must always be preserved.

# Version History Summary

The Version History provides the official governance record for the Warehouse Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

By documenting every approved enhancement, governance decision and structural evolution, Swissbay ensures that the Warehouse Business Object remains transparent, auditable and maintainable throughout its lifecycle while supporting enterprise warehouse management, intelligent logistics and continuous organisational improvement.
