# 18 — Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Warehouse Roadmap defines the long-term strategic evolution of the Warehouse Business Object within Swissbay Nexus.

It provides a structured vision for enhancing warehouse operations, automation, robotics, Artificial Intelligence and supply chain optimisation while maintaining governance, interoperability and backwards compatibility.

---

# Purpose

The Warehouse Roadmap exists to:

- provide strategic direction
- strengthen warehouse operations
- improve supply chain performance
- enable Artificial Intelligence
- support enterprise growth
- improve automation
- maximise warehouse efficiency

The roadmap guides controlled evolution.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Future enhancements should always be:

- business-driven
- reusable
- governed
- secure
- measurable
- AI-ready
- backwards compatible

Every enhancement should improve warehouse operations.

# Roadmap Summary

The Warehouse Roadmap provides the long-term strategic vision for enterprise warehouse management within Swissbay Nexus.

By progressively introducing robotics, IoT, Digital Twins, predictive logistics and intelligent warehouse optimisation, Swissbay ensures that the Warehouse Business Object evolves while remaining governed, secure and strategically aligned.
