# 14 — Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

---

# Overview

The Warehouse KPI Model defines the official performance measurements used to evaluate enterprise warehouse operations within Swissbay Nexus.

KPIs provide consistent, governed metrics for measuring warehouse efficiency, inventory accuracy, fulfilment performance, operational effectiveness and supply chain performance.

Every KPI follows the Swissbay KPI Standard (KPI-000).

---

# Purpose

The Warehouse KPI Model exists to:

- measure warehouse performance
- improve inventory accuracy
- optimise warehouse operations
- strengthen governance
- improve fulfilment
- support Artificial Intelligence
- maximise operational value

KPIs measure business outcomes rather than operational activity.

# KPI-WH-001 — Warehouse Utilisation

## Formula

```text
Occupied Capacity

÷

Maximum Capacity

×

100
```

Measures warehouse capacity usage.

---

# KPI Validation

Before approval verify:

- KPI purpose documented
- calculation defined
- owner assigned
- reporting frequency defined
- measurable business value demonstrated

# KPI Summary

The Warehouse KPI Portfolio provides the official measurements for evaluating enterprise warehouse operations within Swissbay Nexus.
