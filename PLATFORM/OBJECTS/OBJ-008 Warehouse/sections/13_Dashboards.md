# 13 — Dashboards

---

# Overview

The Warehouse Dashboard Portfolio provides operational, tactical and executive visibility into warehouse operations across Swissbay Nexus.

Dashboards transform governed Warehouse information into actionable intelligence that supports warehouse operations, supply chain management, logistics, finance, executive governance and Artificial Intelligence.

Dashboards consume Warehouse information.

They never own or modify Warehouse data.

---

# Purpose

Warehouse dashboards exist to:

- monitor warehouse operations
- improve inventory visibility
- strengthen governance
- optimise warehouse utilisation
- improve fulfilment performance
- support executive decision-making
- enable Artificial Intelligence

Dashboards answer business questions rather than simply displaying information.

# DASH-WH-001 — Warehouse Operations Dashboard

## Purpose

Provides operational visibility into warehouse activities.

### Business Questions

- What is the current warehouse utilisation?
- Which storage zones are approaching capacity?
- Which goods were received today?
- Which goods were issued today?
- Are warehouse operations performing as expected?

### KPIs

- Warehouse Utilisation
- Goods Receipt Accuracy
- Goods Issue Accuracy
- Inventory Accuracy
- Picking Performance

---

# DASH-WH-002 — Executive Supply Chain Dashboard

# AI Integration

Artificial Intelligence enhances dashboards by:

- forecasting demand
- identifying bottlenecks
- recommending replenishment
- optimising layouts
- generating executive summaries

Every recommendation includes supporting evidence and confidence scores.

---

# Dashboard Governance

Every dashboard should:

- consume governed Warehouse information
- display governed KPIs
- respect role-based security
- maintain auditability
- preserve consistent business definitions

Dashboards never redefine business calculations.

# Dashboard Summary

The Warehouse Dashboard Portfolio transforms governed Warehouse information into enterprise intelligence.

By combining warehouse visibility, inventory monitoring, logistics performance, capacity analysis and AI-powered optimisation, Swissbay Nexus enables organisations to maximise warehouse efficiency while maintaining governance, operational excellence and supply chain resilience.
