# 12 — AI Usage

---

# Overview

Artificial Intelligence enhances the Warehouse Business Object by providing intelligent forecasting, warehouse optimisation, inventory analysis, anomaly detection and operational recommendations throughout the warehouse lifecycle.

AI supports Warehouse Managers, Supply Chain Teams, Logistics, Procurement and Executives by transforming governed warehouse information into actionable operational intelligence.

AI never replaces operational responsibility, inventory governance or executive accountability.

All AI capabilities operate in accordance with the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000).

---

# Purpose

The AI Usage Model exists to:

- optimise warehouse operations
- improve inventory visibility
- reduce warehouse costs
- improve fulfilment performance
- strengthen governance
- support executive decision-making
- improve operational resilience
- enable intelligent warehouse management

AI augments people rather than replacing them.

---

# AI Principles

Artificial Intelligence within Warehouse Management follows these principles:

- explainable
- governed
- transparent
- auditable
- secure
- human supervised
- business-first

Recommendations must always include supporting evidence.

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI supports warehouse operations by:

- forecasting inventory demand
- predicting stock shortages
- recommending replenishment
- optimising warehouse layouts
- improving storage allocation
- optimising picking routes
- analysing warehouse utilisation

AI assists decision-making while preserving accountability.

---

# AI Capabilities

## Demand Forecasting

AI analyses:

- historical inventory usage
- sales trends
- seasonal demand
- project requirements
- supplier lead times

Outputs include inventory forecasts and replenishment recommendations.

---

## Warehouse Layout Optimisation

AI evaluates:

- storage utilisation
- travel distance
- picking frequency
- warehouse congestion
- inventory movement

Outputs include layout improvement recommendations.

---

## Picking Route Optimisation

AI identifies:

- shortest travel paths
- optimal picking sequences
- workload balancing
- congestion reduction

Improves warehouse productivity.

---

## Capacity Forecasting

AI estimates:

- future storage requirements
- warehouse utilisation trends
- overflow risk
- expansion requirements

Supports long-term warehouse planning.

---

## Inventory Risk Analysis

AI evaluates:

- slow-moving stock
- obsolete inventory
- stock-out risk
- overstock risk
- replenishment timing

Supports proactive inventory management.

---

## Executive Summaries

AI automatically prepares:

- warehouse performance summaries
- inventory trends
- capacity forecasts
- fulfilment performance
- operational risks

Summaries support governance meetings and executive reporting.

---

# AI Inputs

Artificial Intelligence consumes:

- Warehouse Status
- Capacity Information
- Storage Locations
- Inventory Movements
- Warehouse Utilisation
- Operational Metrics
- Relationships
- Audit History

Only validated and governed information may be consumed.

---

# AI Outputs

AI may generate:

- replenishment recommendations
- warehouse optimisation plans
- layout recommendations
- utilisation reports
- bottleneck alerts
- executive summaries
- operational forecasts

Outputs remain linked to supporting evidence.

---

# Human Oversight

The following decisions remain exclusively human:

- warehouse establishment
- warehouse closure
- capacity approval
- inventory write-offs
- governance approval
- policy exceptions

AI recommendations are advisory only.

---

# Explainability

Every AI recommendation should include:

- recommendation
- evidence
- confidence score
- contributing factors
- recommended actions

Users should understand why AI reached each conclusion.

---

# Governance

AI usage requires:

- approved models
- governed datasets
- audit logging
- security enforcement
- human accountability

AI activity must remain fully traceable.

---

# AI Summary

The AI Usage Model transforms the Warehouse Business Object into an intelligent enterprise capability.

By combining governed warehouse information with predictive analytics, explainable recommendations and operational optimisation, Swissbay Nexus improves warehouse efficiency, inventory visibility, fulfilment performance and supply chain resilience while preserving governance and human accountability.
