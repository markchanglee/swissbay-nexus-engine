# 14 — Mining KPIs

---

# Overview

The Mining KPI Framework defines how organisational performance is measured across the Swissbay Mining Enterprise Platform.

KPIs are derived from canonical Business Objects, enterprise workflows, operational telemetry and AI insights.

The framework provides a single, governed source of truth for operational, financial, environmental and strategic performance.

---

# Purpose

The Mining KPI Framework exists to:

- measure enterprise performance
- improve operational visibility
- support executive decision-making
- strengthen governance
- enable benchmarking
- drive continuous improvement

KPIs become enterprise assets rather than isolated reports.

---

# KPI Philosophy

Swissbay follows one principle.

> **Every strategic objective must have measurable outcomes.**

Measurement drives improvement.

---

# KPI Domains

Swissbay organises KPIs into the following domains.

---

## Executive KPIs

Examples:

- revenue
- EBITDA
- operating cost
- production achievement
- project delivery
- return on capital

Audience:

Board and executive management.

---

## Exploration KPIs

Examples:

- drilling metres completed
- exploration spend
- discovery rate
- resource conversion
- drilling productivity

---

## Production KPIs

Examples:

- tonnes mined
- tonnes processed
- plant utilisation
- equipment availability
- recovery percentage
- production variance

---

## Maintenance KPIs

Examples:

- Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF)
- Mean Time To Repair (MTTR)
- planned maintenance ratio
- equipment uptime
- maintenance backlog

---

## Environmental KPIs

Examples:

- water consumption
- rehabilitation progress
- emissions
- waste management
- environmental incidents

---

## Safety KPIs

Examples:

- Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR)
- Total Recordable Injury Frequency Rate (TRIFR)
- near misses
- safety observations
- training compliance

---

## Community KPIs

Examples:

- local employment
- procurement from local suppliers
- community investment
- grievance resolution time
- SLP milestone completion

---

## Compliance KPIs

Examples:

- licence renewals
- audit findings
- corrective action closure
- permit compliance
- reporting timeliness

---

## AI KPIs

Examples:

- AI adoption
- recommendation acceptance
- productivity gains
- agent utilisation
- response quality
- model accuracy

# KPI Governance

Every KPI defines:

- owner
- business purpose
- calculation method
- source Business Objects
- reporting frequency
- target
- threshold

This ensures consistency across the organisation.

---

# AI Integration

AI supports KPI management by:

- identifying anomalies
- explaining performance changes
- forecasting trends
- recommending corrective actions
- generating executive narratives

Analytics become actionable.

---

# Architectural Decisions

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Shared KPI framework | Enterprise consistency |
| Business Objects as data source | Trusted metrics |
| AI interpretation | Improved executive insights |
| Governed calculations | Comparable reporting |
| Executive dashboards | Strategic visibility |

# Summary

The Mining KPI Framework establishes a consistent approach to measuring mining performance.

By combining Business Objects, enterprise workflows, operational data and AI-driven insights, Swissbay enables organisations to monitor performance, improve decision-making and drive continuous operational excellence.
