# Overview

This document records the architectural evolution of the Swissbay Mining Enterprise Platform.

Maintaining a structured version history ensures traceability, supports governance and provides a foundation for the continued evolution of the Mining Enterprise Platform.

The Mining Enterprise Platform extends the Swissbay Enterprise Operating System and therefore follows the same principles of architectural stability, controlled evolution and backwards compatibility.

# Summary

The Version History documents the continued evolution of the Swissbay Mining Enterprise Platform.

By recording architectural milestones, planned enhancements and governance decisions, Swissbay ensures that the Mining Enterprise Platform evolves in a structured, transparent and sustainable manner while remaining aligned with the long-term vision of the Enterprise Operating System.
