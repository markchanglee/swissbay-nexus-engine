# 03 — Mining Business Model

---

# Overview

The Mining Business Model defines the business domains, capabilities and value streams that comprise a modern mining enterprise.

Rather than modelling mining as isolated departments, Swissbay represents mining as an interconnected enterprise built upon canonical Business Objects, governed Platform Services and AI-native operational capabilities.

The business model provides a common language for mining organisations regardless of commodity, geography or organisational size.

---

# Purpose

The Mining Business Model exists to:

- define mining business capabilities
- standardise operational terminology
- align enterprise architecture with mining operations
- simplify digital transformation
- enable cross-functional collaboration
- support AI reasoning
- provide a reusable operating model

# Core Business Domains

Swissbay groups mining activities into the following domains.

---

## Exploration

Responsible for:

- geological surveys
- geophysical surveys
- geochemical sampling
- drilling programmes
- resource modelling

Primary outcome:

Discovery of economically viable mineral resources.

---

## Prospecting

Responsible for:

- prospecting rights
- land access
- exploration planning
- licence management

Primary outcome:

Identification and validation of exploration opportunities.

---

## Mineral Rights Management

Responsible for:

- prospecting rights
- mining rights
- permits
- renewals
- regulatory submissions

Primary outcome:

Secure and maintain legal authority to operate.

---

## Project Development

Responsible for:

- feasibility studies
- environmental assessments
- engineering
- capital planning
- construction

Primary outcome:

Prepare projects for production.

---

## Mine Operations

Responsible for:

- drilling
- blasting
- loading
- hauling
- production scheduling
- operational monitoring

Primary outcome:

Safe and efficient mineral extraction.

---

## Processing

Responsible for:

- crushing
- milling
- concentration
- beneficiation
- recovery optimisation

Primary outcome:

Convert ore into marketable products.

---

## Supply Chain

Responsible for:

- procurement
- inventory
- contractor management
- warehouse operations
- logistics

Primary outcome:

Reliable operational support.

---

## Sales & Marketing

Responsible for:

- contracts
- commodity sales
- customer management
- exports
- commercial reporting

Primary outcome:

Deliver products to market.

---

## Sustainability

Responsible for:

- ESG
- environmental management
- rehabilitation
- water management
- biodiversity
- emissions

Primary outcome:

Responsible resource development.

---

## Corporate Services

Responsible for:

- finance
- HR
- legal
- governance
- IT
- risk management

Primary outcome:

Enterprise enablement.

# Enterprise Value Streams

Swissbay identifies six major value streams.

1. Discover Resources
2. Secure Mining Rights
3. Develop Mining Projects
4. Extract Minerals
5. Process and Deliver Products
6. Close and Rehabilitate Operations

Every workflow contributes to one or more value streams.

AI augments every stage of the mining lifecycle.

# Summary

The Mining Business Model provides a unified representation of how modern mining organisations operate.

By organising mining into interconnected business domains supported by shared Business Objects, Platform Services and AI capabilities, Swissbay establishes a scalable operating model suitable for junior miners, multinational producers and government-owned enterprises alike.
