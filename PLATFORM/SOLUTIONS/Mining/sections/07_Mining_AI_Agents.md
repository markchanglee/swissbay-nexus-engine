# 07 — Mining AI Agents

---

# Overview

The Mining AI Agents extend the Swissbay AI Enterprise Platform with mining-specific intelligence.

Rather than introducing isolated chatbots, Swissbay deploys specialised enterprise agents that collaborate across exploration, operations, environmental management, compliance and executive decision-making.

Every Mining AI Agent shares the same AI Runtime, Enterprise Memory, Knowledge Graph, Business Objects and Platform Services.

Only domain expertise changes.

---

# Purpose

Mining AI Agents exist to:

- improve operational decisions
- reduce manual analysis
- support mining professionals
- automate repetitive knowledge work
- preserve organisational expertise
- improve compliance
- optimise production

Agents become digital members of the mining organisation.

# AI Capabilities

Mining agents may:

- reason
- summarise
- recommend
- classify
- forecast
- retrieve enterprise knowledge
- coordinate workflows

Mining agents do not independently approve regulated activities.

---

# Human Oversight

The following always require human approval:

- mining right submissions
- environmental approvals
- production commitments
- financial approvals
- rehabilitation certification
- regulatory reporting

AI provides decision support rather than replacing accountable professionals.

---

# Architectural Decisions

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Shared AI Runtime | Consistent intelligence |
| Specialist agents | Domain expertise |
| Multi-agent collaboration | Better decisions |
| Platform Services execute actions | Preserves governance |
| Human oversight | Regulatory compliance |
