# 08 — Mining Analytics

---

# Overview

Mining Analytics provides enterprise-wide visibility into mining operations through governed reporting, predictive insights and executive dashboards.

Rather than producing isolated reports, Swissbay combines Business Objects, Enterprise Memory, the Knowledge Graph, operational telemetry and AI reasoning into one analytics platform.

Analytics become a strategic capability that supports operational excellence and executive decision-making.

---

# Purpose

Mining Analytics exists to:

- improve operational visibility
- support strategic planning
- optimise production
- strengthen governance
- enable predictive insights
- simplify regulatory reporting
- provide executive intelligence

Analytics transform operational data into enterprise knowledge.
