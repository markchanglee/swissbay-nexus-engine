# Overview

The Swissbay Mining Enterprise Platform is the flagship industry implementation of the Swissbay Enterprise Operating System.

Rather than creating an isolated mining application, the platform extends the Foundation, Canonical Business Objects, Nexus Platform, Swissbay Enterprise Reference Architecture (SERA) and AI Enterprise Platform with mining-specific capabilities.

Every mining solution remains aligned with the core Swissbay architecture while introducing specialised business models, workflows, integrations and Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Purpose

The Mining Enterprise Platform exists to:

- digitise mining operations
- simplify regulatory compliance
- improve operational efficiency
- provide enterprise-wide visibility
- strengthen governance

Swissbay provides one integrated operating platform for the mining industry.
