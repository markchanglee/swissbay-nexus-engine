# 14 — Evaluation Framework

---

# Overview

The Evaluation Framework defines how the Swissbay AI Enterprise Platform measures the quality, reliability and business value of every AI capability.

Evaluation is not treated as a one-time activity performed before deployment.

Instead, every model, prompt, agent, workflow and enterprise interaction is continuously assessed to ensure that AI remains trustworthy, effective and aligned with organisational objectives.

Evaluation becomes a permanent enterprise capability.

---

# Purpose

The Evaluation Framework exists to:

- measure AI quality
- detect performance degradation
- reduce hallucinations
- improve business outcomes
- support governance
- optimise operational costs
- strengthen enterprise trust

Every AI capability is measurable.

---

# Evaluation Philosophy

Swissbay follows one principle.

> **If enterprise intelligence cannot be measured, it cannot be trusted.**

Continuous evaluation is mandatory.

---

# Evaluation Scope

Swissbay evaluates:

### Models

Accuracy, latency, cost, robustness.

---

### Prompts

Consistency, relevance, clarity.

---

### Agents

Goal completion, planning quality, collaboration.

---

### Tools

Execution success, latency, failures.

---

### Retrieval

Context relevance, citation quality.

---

### Responses

Correctness, explainability, usefulness.

---

### Business Outcomes

Operational improvement, efficiency, user satisfaction.

---

# Evaluation Metrics

Key metrics include:

- factual accuracy
- hallucination rate
- citation coverage
- reasoning quality
- response relevance
- task completion
- latency
- token usage
- operational cost
- user satisfaction

Metrics may be extended according to industry requirements.

# Architectural Decisions

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Evaluation is continuous | Sustains quality |
| Business outcomes are measured | Aligns with enterprise value |
| Human review complements automation | Preserves accountability |
| Benchmark suites are standardised | Enables comparison |
| Evaluation informs Runtime decisions | Creates adaptive intelligence |

# Design Principles

The Evaluation Framework should remain:

- measurable
- transparent
- repeatable
- explainable
- governed
- business-focused

Evaluation exists to improve enterprise intelligence rather than merely report statistics.

The Evaluation Framework defines how Swissbay measures enterprise intelligence.

By continuously evaluating models, prompts, agents, retrieval, tools and business outcomes, Swissbay creates a trusted AI platform capable of improving over time while maintaining governance, accountability and measurable enterprise value.
