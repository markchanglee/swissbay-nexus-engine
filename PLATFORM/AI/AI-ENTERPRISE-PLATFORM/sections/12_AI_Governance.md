# 12 — AI Governance

---

# Overview

The AI Governance Architecture defines how Artificial Intelligence is managed, controlled and continuously improved across the Swissbay Enterprise Platform.

AI Governance extends traditional enterprise governance by introducing policies, accountability, lifecycle management and oversight for models, prompts, agents, memory, tools and AI-generated outcomes.

Governance ensures AI remains aligned with organisational objectives throughout its lifecycle.

---

# Purpose

The AI Governance Architecture exists to:

- establish accountability
- manage AI risk
- preserve trust
- support regulatory compliance
- govern AI assets
- ensure responsible AI adoption
- enable continuous improvement

Governance provides confidence in enterprise AI.

---

# Governance Philosophy

Swissbay follows one principle.

> **Every AI capability must have an owner, a purpose and measurable accountability.**

Unowned AI is unmanaged AI.

Regular reviews assess:

- architectural compliance
