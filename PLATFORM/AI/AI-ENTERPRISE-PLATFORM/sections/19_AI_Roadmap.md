# 19 — AI Roadmap

---

# Overview

The AI Roadmap defines the strategic evolution of the Swissbay AI Enterprise Platform.

It describes how the platform will mature from governed enterprise intelligence into a comprehensive ecosystem of collaborative agents, autonomous planning and enterprise optimisation.

The roadmap guides long-term investment while preserving the canonical architecture.

---

# Strategic Vision

Swissbay AI evolves through six capability horizons.

# Guiding Principles

Every stage must:

- preserve governance
- remain explainable
- support human accountability
- reuse Business Objects
- integrate with Platform Services
- remain provider independent

---

# Success Measures

Swissbay AI succeeds when:

- AI improves enterprise decisions
- Business Objects remain authoritative
- organisations trust AI recommendations
- Industry Agents share one runtime
- governance scales with intelligence

---

# Summary

The AI Roadmap provides the long-term direction of Swissbay AI.

It ensures that innovation strengthens the Enterprise Operating System rather than creating isolated AI capabilities.
