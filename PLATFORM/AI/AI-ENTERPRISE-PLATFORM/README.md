# Overview

The Swissbay AI Enterprise Platform is the intelligence layer of the Swissbay Enterprise Operating System.

It provides governed Artificial Intelligence capabilities across every Business Object, Platform Service, Industry Pack, Application and Enterprise Workflow.

Unlike traditional AI implementations that are added as isolated assistants or standalone tools, Swissbay treats AI as a shared enterprise runtime capability.

AI operates through governed services, agents, tools, memory, knowledge, evaluation, observability and security.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of the AI Enterprise Platform is to provide a unified architecture for enterprise intelligence.

It enables Swissbay to support:

- AI copilots
- AI agents
- recommendations
- predictions
- summarisation
- reasoning
- planning
- retrieval-augmented generation
- enterprise memory
- semantic search
- automated analysis
- industry-specific intelligence

AI becomes a reusable capability rather than a duplicated feature.

---

# Vision

Swissbay AI is designed to become the intelligence fabric of the enterprise.

It should allow organisations to ask, reason, decide and act across their business using governed AI connected to canonical Business Objects and Platform Services.

The long-term vision is:

```text
Enterprise Knowledge

↓

Enterprise Memory

↓

Enterprise Reasoning

↓

Enterprise Action
```

AI should improve decision-making while preserving human accountability.

---

# AI Philosophy

Swissbay AI follows four core principles.

## AI Assists

AI supports users, teams and organisations.

It does not replace accountable decision-makers.

---

## AI Explains

Every recommendation, prediction or generated output should be explainable.

Users should understand why AI reached a conclusion.

---

## AI Obeys

AI must operate within governance, security, business rules and approval boundaries.

AI never bypasses the platform.

---

## AI Learns

AI improves over time through governed feedback, evaluation and monitoring.

Learning must remain controlled, auditable and safe.

# Human Accountability

The AI Enterprise Platform preserves human authority.

AI may recommend, summarise, classify, predict and assist.

AI may not independently:

- approve contracts
- make legal decisions
- make employment decisions
- approve payments
- override business rules
- bypass governance
- execute restricted actions

Critical decisions remain human-governed.
