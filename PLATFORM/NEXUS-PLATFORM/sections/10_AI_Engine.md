# 10 — AI Engine

---

# Overview

The AI Engine is the intelligence capability of the Swissbay Nexus Platform.

It provides reusable Artificial Intelligence services that are available to every Platform Service, Business Object and enterprise application.

Rather than embedding AI independently within individual solutions, Swissbay centralises intelligence into a governed runtime capability that delivers recommendations, predictions, reasoning and natural language interaction across the platform.

The AI Engine augments human decision-making while preserving governance and accountability.

---

# Purpose

The AI Engine exists to:

- provide enterprise intelligence
- improve decision-making
- support automation
- enhance productivity
- enable predictive analytics
- power AI agents
- strengthen enterprise knowledge
- maximise organisational insight

AI is treated as a shared enterprise capability rather than an isolated feature.

# AI Operating Principles

Artificial Intelligence within Swissbay must be:

- explainable
- governed
- secure
- transparent
- auditable
- accountable
- human supervised

Every recommendation should include supporting evidence and a confidence score.

---

# AI Inputs

The AI Engine consumes governed information from:

- Business Objects
- Knowledge Graph
- Metadata Registry
- Event Bus
- Audit Service
- Platform Services
- Enterprise Documents

Only trusted and validated information is used.

---

# AI Outputs

The AI Engine may produce:

- recommendations
- predictions
- summaries
- classifications
- confidence scores
- alerts
- insights
- reasoning traces

Outputs remain advisory unless explicitly incorporated into governed workflows.

---

# Human Authority

The following responsibilities remain exclusively human:

- legal approval
- financial approval
- employment decisions
- contract execution
- governance approval
- policy changes

The AI Engine supports people—it does not replace them.

---

# AI Governance

Every AI capability requires:

- approved models
- governed datasets
- audit logging
- version control
- performance monitoring
- bias evaluation
- explainability

AI operates under the Swissbay AI Operating Standard (AI-000).

---

# Monitoring

The AI Engine monitors:

- inference volume
- response latency
- confidence trends
- model accuracy
- recommendation adoption
- drift detection
- operational health

Continuous monitoring ensures trusted AI behaviour.

---

# Design Principles

The AI Engine should be:

- model independent
- provider independent
- explainable
- observable
- secure
- extensible
- reusable

Swissbay should support multiple AI providers without changing the platform architecture.
