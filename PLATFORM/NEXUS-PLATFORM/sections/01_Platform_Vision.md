# 01 — Platform Vision

---

# Overview

The Swissbay Nexus Platform is the enterprise runtime architecture that powers every capability within Swissbay.

Unlike traditional enterprise software, which is typically built around individual applications, Swissbay is designed as a **platform-first architecture** where business capabilities execute through a shared runtime.

The platform provides common enterprise services including identity, workflow orchestration, business rules, validation, automation, artificial intelligence, event processing, integration, search, reporting and governance.

Business Objects describe enterprise knowledge.

The Nexus Platform brings that knowledge to life.

# The Problem

Modern enterprises commonly operate dozens of disconnected systems.

Examples include:

- CRM
- ERP
- HR
- Procurement
- Asset Management
- Project Management
- Finance
- Document Management
- Reporting
- AI Platforms

Each system duplicates:

- workflows
- validation
- security
- integrations
- automation
- reporting
- business rules

The result is:

- inconsistent behaviour
- duplicated logic
- high maintenance costs
- fragmented data
- difficult integrations
- slow innovation

---

# The Swissbay Approach

Swissbay separates **business knowledge** from **platform behaviour**.

Instead of embedding business logic inside applications, Swissbay centralises enterprise behaviour into reusable platform services.

Business Objects remain lightweight, governed representations of enterprise concepts.

Platform services execute the behaviour.

```text
Business Objects

↓

Platform Services

↓

Enterprise Applications
```

This architecture enables every application to behave consistently while sharing one enterprise operating model.

---

# Platform Philosophy

Swissbay is built on five fundamental beliefs.

## 1. Business Before Technology

Technology exists to support business capabilities.

Business architecture should never depend on implementation technology.

---

## 2. Platform Before Applications

Applications consume platform capabilities.

Applications should not reimplement workflow engines, validation engines or AI.

---

## 3. AI as a Native Capability

Artificial Intelligence is not an add-on.

It is a core runtime capability available to every Business Object.

---

## 4. Events Before Integrations

Business events become the primary communication mechanism.

Systems react to enterprise events instead of tightly coupled point-to-point integrations.

---

## 5. Governance Everywhere

Every action performed within Swissbay is governed, traceable and auditable.

Governance is a platform capability—not an afterthought.
