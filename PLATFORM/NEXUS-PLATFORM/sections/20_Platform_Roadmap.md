# 20 — Platform Roadmap

---

# Overview

The Platform Roadmap defines the strategic evolution of the Swissbay Nexus Platform.

It provides a long-term vision for expanding the platform through intelligent services, advanced Artificial Intelligence, enterprise automation and industry-specific capabilities while preserving the canonical Business Object model and architectural principles.

The roadmap ensures controlled innovation without compromising platform stability.

---

# Purpose

The Platform Roadmap exists to:

- guide platform evolution
- prioritise strategic capabilities
- support enterprise growth
- improve developer productivity
- strengthen Artificial Intelligence
- expand industry support
- preserve architectural integrity

The roadmap aligns platform investment with long-term business value.

# Platform Success Measures

The platform is successful when:

- new solutions require minimal custom infrastructure
- Business Objects remain unchanged across industries
- platform services are reused consistently
- AI adoption improves decision-making
- integrations are standardised
- deployment time decreases
- governance remains consistent

---

# Technology Strategy

Swissbay remains:

- cloud agnostic
- database agnostic
- AI provider agnostic
- deployment agnostic
- programming language agnostic

Technology choices may evolve without changing the architecture.

---

# Governance

Roadmap updates require:

- Enterprise Architecture Review
- Governance Board Approval
- Platform Version Update
- Strategic Review

The roadmap should be reviewed annually.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Swissbay aims to become:

> **The enterprise operating system for intelligent organisations.**

Organisations should be able to build industry-specific solutions by combining:

- Canonical Business Objects
- Platform Services
- AI Capabilities
- Industry Packs
- SDKs
- Governance Standards

without redesigning the underlying architecture.

---

# Summary

The Platform Roadmap provides the long-term strategic direction for the Swissbay Nexus Platform.

By progressively expanding runtime services, enterprise AI, industry extensions and developer capabilities while preserving the canonical architecture, Swissbay establishes a sustainable foundation for intelligent enterprise platforms across multiple industries.
