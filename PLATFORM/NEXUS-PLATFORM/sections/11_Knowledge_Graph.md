# 11 — Knowledge Graph

---

# Overview

The Knowledge Graph is the semantic intelligence layer of the Swissbay Nexus Platform.

Rather than storing isolated Business Objects, the Knowledge Graph represents the enterprise as an interconnected network of entities, relationships, events and business concepts.

It enables Artificial Intelligence, enterprise search, impact analysis and intelligent reasoning by understanding how information is connected rather than simply where it is stored.

The Knowledge Graph never replaces Business Objects.

Business Objects remain the authoritative source of enterprise information.

The Knowledge Graph provides semantic context.

---

# Purpose

The Knowledge Graph exists to:

- model enterprise relationships
- support Artificial Intelligence
- enable semantic search
- improve enterprise reasoning
- support impact analysis
- strengthen governance
- provide organisational context

Knowledge transforms information into understanding.

# Enterprise Reasoning

The Knowledge Graph enables questions such as:

- Which contracts are affected by this supplier?
- Which customers depend on this warehouse?
- What projects use this asset?
- What business units are impacted by this regulation?
- Which opportunities originated from this customer?

Answers are derived through relationships rather than procedural logic.

---

# AI Integration

The AI Engine consumes graph context to:

- improve recommendations
- understand enterprise relationships
- generate better summaries
- perform semantic reasoning
- identify hidden dependencies

AI reasoning becomes context-aware rather than document-aware.

---

# Search Integration

The Search Engine uses the Knowledge Graph for:

- semantic search
- relationship discovery
- contextual navigation
- entity expansion
- concept linking

Users search for meaning instead of keywords.

---

# Governance

Every graph relationship must:

- reference canonical Business Objects
- remain auditable
- be versioned
- preserve lineage
- support explainability

The Knowledge Graph never invents enterprise facts.

---

# Design Principles

The Knowledge Graph should be:

- semantic
- explainable
- governed
- extensible
- technology independent
- AI-ready

Relationships are first-class enterprise assets.

# Summary

The Knowledge Graph provides the semantic intelligence layer of the Swissbay Nexus Platform.

By modelling relationships between Business Objects, it enables explainable AI, semantic search, enterprise reasoning and impact analysis while preserving the canonical authority of the underlying Business Objects.
