# 08 — AI Architecture

---

# Overview

The AI Architecture defines how Artificial Intelligence is embedded throughout the Swissbay Enterprise Operating System.

Swissbay does not treat AI as an external assistant or isolated feature.

Instead, Artificial Intelligence operates as a governed enterprise capability integrated into every architectural layer.

The AI Architecture ensures that intelligence is reusable, explainable, secure and consistent across all Business Objects, Platform Services and enterprise applications.

---

# Purpose

The AI Architecture exists to:

- embed intelligence throughout the platform
- support enterprise decision-making
- augment human capability
- strengthen automation
- enable semantic reasoning
- preserve governance
- standardise AI operations

Artificial Intelligence becomes an enterprise service rather than an application feature.

---

# AI Philosophy

Swissbay follows four principles.

### AI Assists

AI supports people.

It does not replace authorised decision-makers.

---

### AI Learns

AI continuously improves using governed enterprise information.

---

### AI Explains

Every recommendation should include supporting evidence and confidence.

---

### AI Obeys

AI always operates within enterprise governance.
